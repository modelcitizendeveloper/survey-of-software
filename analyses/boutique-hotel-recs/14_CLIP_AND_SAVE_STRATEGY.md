## Why This Works

### 1. Zero Fulfillment Cost (For You)

**Traditional demo:**
- Print sheet: $1-2
- Ship to hotel: $5-8
- **Total: $6-10 per hotel**

**Clip-and-save ad:**
- Print full-page ad: $3,000-5,000 per issue

**Problem with mass demos:**

## Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Low Clip Rate

**Problem:** Only 0.2% clip (vs expected 1-2%)

**Causes:**
- Ad not compelling enough
- Template too complex
- Unclear value proposition

**Mitigation:**
- A/B test headlines and copy
- Simplify template (10 spots â†’ 5 spots)
- Add testimonial in ad ("We saved 40 hours/month!")

---

### Risk 2: High Clip, Low Activation

**Problem:** Hotels clip but don't scan setup QR

**Causes:**
- Forgot to activate
- Don't understand how
- Lost clipped ad

**Mitigation:**
- Make setup QR larger + more prominent
- Add instructions: "STEP 1: Scan this QR before laminating"
- Email follow-up: "Did you clip our ad? Activate here [link]"

---

### Risk 3: High Activation, Low Upgrade

**Problem:** Hotels activate but never upgrade to paid

**Causes:**
- Free tier is "good enough"
- Don't see value in custom branding
- Forgot about it after 30 days

**Mitigation:**
- Email sequence: Day 7, 14, 21, 28 (engagement + upgrade prompts)
- Show ROI in admin panel: "You saved 40 hours this month = $800"
- Limit free tier more aggressively (30 scans instead of 100)
