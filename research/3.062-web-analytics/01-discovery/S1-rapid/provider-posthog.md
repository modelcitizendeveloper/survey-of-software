# PostHog

**Category:** Self-hosted / Full-featured / Developer-focused
**Website:** https://posthog.com/
**Pricing:** Free tier (1M events/month), then $0.00045/event

## Key Features
- All-in-one platform: analytics, session replay, feature flags, A/B testing
- Generous free tier (1M events + 5K recordings monthly)
- Self-hosted MIT license option available
- Developer-focused with strong API
- Product analytics focus (apps and products vs websites)

## Best For
Product teams and startups building apps/SaaS who need analytics combined with feature flags, experiments, and session replay in one platform.

## Quick Notes
- Often 30-50% cheaper than SaaS competitors at scale
- Self-hosted version has limited features (pushes to cloud)
- Complex deployment (multiple containers)
- Focuses more on product analytics than website analytics
- Popular in developer/startup ecosystem
