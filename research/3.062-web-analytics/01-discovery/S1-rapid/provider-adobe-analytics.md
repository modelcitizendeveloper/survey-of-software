# Adobe Analytics

**Category:** Full-featured / Enterprise
**Website:** https://business.adobe.com/products/analytics/adobe-analytics.html
**Pricing:** Custom enterprise pricing (contact sales)

## Key Features
- Part of Adobe Experience Platform ecosystem
- Advanced segmentation and attribution modeling
- Real-time analytics and dashboards
- Predictive analytics and AI-powered insights
- Deep integration with Adobe Creative Cloud and Marketing Cloud

## Best For
Large enterprises and marketing organizations already invested in Adobe ecosystem, requiring sophisticated marketing analytics and attribution.

## Quick Notes
- Traditional enterprise analytics platform
- Positioned as Google Analytics 360 competitor
- Requires significant investment and expertise
- Complex implementation and learning curve
- Strong in marketing/advertising use cases
