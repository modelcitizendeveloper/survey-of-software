## Executive Summary

**Key Finding**: Feature coverage varies dramatically - Motion = all-in-one, Reclaim = integration-focused, Trevor = visual-first.

**Best for each category**:
- **Calendar integrations**: Reclaim.ai (Google + Outlook + sync)
- **Task source integrations**: Reclaim.ai (Todoist, Asana, Linear, Jira, ClickUp)
- **Mobile app quality**: Motion (iOS ⭐⭐⭐⭐½, Android ⭐⭐⭐⭐)
- **Team features**: Motion (workload balancing, shared projects)
- **All-in-one**: Motion (tasks + calendar + projects + meetings)
- **Value**: Reclaim.ai (most features at $8-12/month)

## Mobile App Deep-Dive

### iOS App Comparison

**Motion** (⭐⭐⭐⭐½ - 4.5/5)
- **Pros**: Full calendar view, task editing, AI scheduling works, offline mode
- **Cons**: Slightly slower than web, occasional sync delays
- **Best features**: Calendar + task unified view, quick task add, project management
- **App Store rating**: 4.6/5 (1,200+ reviews)

**Reclaim.ai** (⭐⭐⭐½ - 3.5/5)
- **Pros**: Habit scheduling, calendar sync, quick task add
- **Cons**: Desktop experience better, limited task editing, no offline
- **Best features**: Habit management, Slack status sync
- **App Store rating**: 4.2/5 (500+ reviews)

**Trevor AI** (⭐⭐⭐⭐ - 4/5)
- **Pros**: Beautiful time blocking UI, drag-drop, visual planner
- **Cons**: Smaller feature set than web, sync can be slow
- **Best features**: Visual time blocks, daily planner view, Pomodoro timer
- **App Store rating**: 4.7/5 (800+ reviews)

**Akiflow** (⭐⭐⭐ - 3/5)
- **Pros**: Universal inbox, quick capture, keyboard shortcuts
- **Cons**: Mobile feels like afterthought (desktop-first), clunky UI
- **Best features**: Quick task capture, inbox processing
- **App Store rating**: 3.9/5 (300+ reviews)

**Sunsama** (⭐⭐⭐⭐ - 4/5)
- **Pros**: Guided planning ritual, beautiful UI, offline mode
- **Cons**: Limited mobile features (desktop better), slow on older devices
- **Best features**: Daily planning workflow, reflection prompts
- **App Store rating**: 4.5/5 (600+ reviews)

**Todoist** (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ - 5/5)
- **Pros**: Best-in-class mobile app, fast, offline, widgets, quick add
- **Cons**: No AI scheduling (not its purpose)
- **Best features**: Natural language, quick add, widgets, offline
- **App Store rating**: 4.8/5 (150,000+ reviews)

**Things** (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ - 5/5)
- **Pros**: iOS-native, beautiful design, Apple ecosystem integration, fast
- **Cons**: No Android, no AI, Apple-only
- **Best features**: iOS design, widgets, quick entry, shortcuts
- **App Store rating**: 4.9/5 (40,000+ reviews)

## Team Features Deep-Dive

### Motion Teams ($34/user/month)

**Features**:
1. **Shared projects**: Entire team sees project structure, dependencies
2. **Task assignment**: Delegate to teammates, AI schedules on their calendar
3. **Workload balancing**: AI distributes tasks across team (prevents overload)
4. **Team calendar**: See everyone's capacity, find meeting times
5. **Meeting optimization**: AI suggests best times for team meetings
6. **Capacity planning**: "Can we finish this project by Friday?" → AI calculates

**Best for**:
- 5-20 person teams (product, engineering, marketing)
- Project-based work (campaigns, sprints, launches)
- Managers needing workload visibility

**Limitations**:
- Expensive ($1,700/month for 5 users, $6,800 for 20 users)
- Overkill for simple task delegation (Asana/Linear cheaper)

---

### Reclaim.ai Business ($10/user/month for teams)

**Features**:
1. **Smart 1:1s**: AI schedules recurring 1:1s with direct reports
2. **Team calendar**: See who's available for ad-hoc syncs
3. **No-meeting days**: Entire team blocks Fridays for focus
4. **Shared habits**: Team-wide "no meetings before 10 AM" policy
5. **Meeting analytics**: Track team meeting load (% of week in meetings)

**Best for**:
- Managers with 5-10 direct reports
- Calendar-heavy teams (sales, customer success)
- Remote teams (need async coordination)

**Limitations**:
- No shared projects (individual task management)
- No task delegation (just scheduling)

---

### Todoist Business ($8/user/month)

**Features**:
1. **Shared projects**: Team projects with task assignment
2. **Comments & collaboration**: Discuss tasks, attach files
3. **Task assignment**: Delegate to teammates
4. **Team inbox**: Shared task list for incoming requests
5. **Admin controls**: Manage team members, billing

**Best for**:
- Task delegation (no AI scheduling needed)
- Asynchronous teams (distributed, remote)
- Budget-conscious (cheaper than Motion/Reclaim)

**Limitations**:
- No AI scheduling (manual prioritization)
- No calendar optimization

## Integration Ecosystem Comparison

### Motion: All-in-One (Replaces Task System)

**Philosophy**: "Motion is your single task + calendar + project system"

**Integrations**:
- ✅ Google Calendar, Outlook (bidirectional)
- ✅ Gmail (create tasks from email)
- ✅ Slack (notifications)
- ✅ Zoom (meeting links)
- ⚠️ Todoist, Asana (one-time import, no sync)

**Trade-off**:
- ✅ Unified experience (everything in one tool)
- ❌ Lock-in (must migrate tasks from Todoist/Asana)

---

### Reclaim.ai: Integration-First (Augments Existing Tools)

**Philosophy**: "Keep your task system (Todoist, Asana), we just schedule it"

**Integrations**:
- ✅ Google Calendar, Outlook (bidirectional)
- ✅ Todoist (bidirectional sync - tasks stay in Todoist)
- ✅ Asana, Linear, Jira, ClickUp (read tasks, create calendar blocks)
- ✅ Slack (status sync, notifications)

**Trade-off**:
- ✅ No lock-in (tasks stay in source system)
- ⚠️ Complexity (multiple tools to manage)

---

### Trevor AI: Manual Import (Hybrid)

**Philosophy**: "Import tasks daily, manually time-block them"

**Integrations**:
- ✅ Google Calendar, Outlook (bidirectional)
- ✅ Todoist (bidirectional)
- ⚠️ Asana, Notion (import only, no sync)

**Trade-off**:
- ⚠️ Daily import workflow (manual, but flexible)
- ⚠️ Some tasks in Trevor, some in source system (duplication risk)

---

### Akiflow: Universal Inbox (Task Aggregator)

**Philosophy**: "Pull tasks from everywhere, manage in one place"

**Integrations**:
- ✅ Todoist, Asana, Linear, Jira, ClickUp (bidirectional)
- ✅ Gmail, Slack (create tasks from messages)
- ✅ Google Calendar, Outlook (bidirectional)
- ✅ Notion (bidirectional)

**Trade-off**:
- ✅ See all tasks in one place (reduced context-switching)
- ⚠️ Broad permissions (access to Gmail, Slack, multiple task systems)

## Verdict: Feature Coverage by Use Case

### Best All-in-One: Motion
- **Why**: Tasks + calendar + projects + team features in one tool
- **Trade-off**: Expensive ($34/month), lock-in (must migrate tasks)

### Best Integration Ecosystem: Reclaim.ai
- **Why**: Syncs with Todoist, Asana, Linear, Jira, ClickUp (keep existing tools)
- **Trade-off**: Less powerful AI (no project dependencies)

### Best Mobile Experience (iOS): Todoist or Things
- **Why**: Best-in-class mobile apps, fast, offline, widgets
- **Trade-off**: No AI scheduling

### Best Mobile Experience (Android): Todoist or Motion
- **Why**: Todoist = best task app, Motion = best AI scheduling
- **Trade-off**: Todoist has no AI, Motion expensive

### Best Team Features: Motion
- **Why**: Workload balancing, shared projects, capacity planning
- **Trade-off**: Expensive ($34/user/month)

### Best Budget Team Features: Reclaim.ai Business
- **Why**: Smart 1:1s, team calendar, $10/user/month
- **Trade-off**: No shared projects, no task delegation

### Best Visual Workflow: Trevor AI
- **Why**: Drag-drop time blocking, beautiful UI
- **Trade-off**: Less AI power, manual placement

### Best for Mindful Planning: Sunsama
- **Why**: Guided daily planning ritual, reflection prompts
- **Trade-off**: Expensive ($20/month), minimal AI

## Key Insights

1. **Motion = All-in-one, Reclaim = Integration-first**: Choose based on "replace vs augment" preference

2. **Mobile apps vary wildly**: Todoist/Things = best mobile, Motion/Reclaim = desktop-first

3. **Team features rare**: Only Motion and Reclaim offer team plans (Motion more powerful, Reclaim cheaper)

4. **Calendar integrations universal**: All tools support Google + Outlook (Apple Calendar weaker)

5. **Task source integrations**: Reclaim best (Todoist, Asana, Linear, Jira, ClickUp), Motion weakest (import only)

**Recommendation**:
- **Want all-in-one** → Motion (tasks + calendar + projects)
- **Keep existing tools** → Reclaim (integrates with Todoist, Asana)
- **Visual thinker** → Trevor AI (drag-drop time blocking)
- **Budget-conscious** → Reclaim Free or Todoist + manual calendar
