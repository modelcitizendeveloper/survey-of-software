## AI Capabilities

**AI Level**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Most Advanced)

### 1. Project Manager AI
**What**: AI understands project structure, dependencies, and deadlines

**Features**:
- **Dependency-aware**: Task A blocks Task B → AI schedules A first
- **Deadline propagation**: Project due Friday → AI works backwards, schedules subtasks
- **Resource allocation**: Multiple projects → AI balances time across priorities
- **Automatic rescheduling**: Meeting added → AI moves affected tasks

**Example**:
- Add project: "Launch website" (Due: Nov 15, 20 hours work)
- AI breaks down: Design (6h) → Development (10h) → Testing (4h)
- AI schedules: Design Mon-Tue, Dev Wed-Thur, Testing Fri
- Meeting pops up Wed? AI reschedules Dev to Thur-Fri, Testing to next week

---

### 2. Auto-Scheduling Algorithm
**How AI decides when to schedule tasks**:

**Factors considered**:
1. **Deadline urgency**: Due soon = higher priority
2. **Task duration**: Match to available calendar blocks
3. **Your productivity patterns**: Focus work in AM, admin in PM
4. **Meeting buffer**: Back-to-back meetings → add recovery time
5. **Project dependencies**: Blocked tasks wait for blockers
6. **Work hours**: Only schedule within configured work hours (9 AM - 6 PM)

**Flexibility settings**:
- **Hard deadline**: Task MUST complete by date (top priority)
- **Soft deadline**: Prefer by date, can slip if needed
- **ASAP**: Schedule as soon as possible
- **Flexible**: Fill gaps, low priority

---

### 3. Intelligent Rescheduling
**Problem**: Calendar changes (new meetings, tasks take longer)

**Motion's solution**:
- Real-time updates: New meeting → AI instantly reschedules affected tasks
- Cascade rescheduling: Task A delayed → Task B, C, D automatically shift
- Conflict resolution: Double-booked → AI picks highest priority

**Value**: No manual "calendar Tetris" when schedule changes
