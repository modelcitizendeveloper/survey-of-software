# S3: Workload Complexity Matching

**Focus**: Match tool to task volume (<10 tasks vs 30-50+ tasks)

---

## Complexity Tiers

### Tier 1: Simple (<10 tasks/week)

**Examples**: Students, light freelancers, hobbyists, part-time workers

**Recommended**: Reclaim Free ($0/month) or Todoist Free ($0/month)
- **Reclaim Free**: Unlimited habits + 10 tasks/week (perfect for light users)
- **Todoist Free**: Unlimited tasks, 5 projects (no AI scheduling)

**Avoid**: Motion ($34/month overkill), paid tools (unnecessary)

---

### Tier 2: Moderate (10-30 tasks/week)

**Examples**: Full-time knowledge workers, consultants, freelancers

**Recommended**: Reclaim Pro ($8/month) or Trevor AI ($9.99/month)
- **Reclaim Pro**: Unlimited tasks, AI scheduling, calendar optimization
- **Trevor AI**: Visual time blocking, manual control

**When to upgrade from free**: >10 tasks/week (Reclaim Free limit)

---

### Tier 3: Complex (30-50+ tasks/week)

**Examples**: Project managers, founders, managers with multiple projects

**Recommended**: Motion ($34/month)
- **Why**: Project dependencies, deadline propagation, complex scheduling
- **When**: Multiple projects with interdependent tasks

**Alternative**: Reclaim Pro + Todoist ($13/month) if budget-constrained

---

## Decision Matrix

| Tasks/Week | Complexity | Tool | Monthly Cost |
|------------|------------|------|--------------|
| <10 | Simple | Reclaim Free or Todoist Free | $0 |
| 10-20 | Moderate | Reclaim Pro | $8 |
| 20-30 | Moderate-High | Reclaim Pro or Trevor AI | $8-10 |
| 30-50 | Complex | Motion | $34 |
| 50+ | Very Complex | Motion + project management tool | $34+ |

**Rule of thumb**:
- <10 tasks → Free tier
- 10-30 tasks → Reclaim Pro ($8/month)
- 30+ tasks with dependencies → Motion ($34/month)
