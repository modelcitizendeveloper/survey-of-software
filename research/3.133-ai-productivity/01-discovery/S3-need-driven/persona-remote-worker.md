**Persona**: Remote workers needing async communication, focus time, Slack status sync
**Recommendation**: Reclaim.ai Pro ($8/month)

## Persona Profile

**Primary needs**:
1. **Slack status sync**: Auto-update status (in meeting, focus time, lunch) - reduce interruptions
2. **Focus time protection**: Block deep work time (prevent Slack/meeting interruptions)
3. **Async-first**: Reduce synchronous meetings, protect maker time
4. **Work-life balance**: Separate work/personal calendars (home office boundaries)
5. **Timezone coordination**: Find meeting times across timezones (if distributed team)

**Pain points**:
- Always-on culture (Slack pings at all hours)
- No commute = meetings fill entire day
- Home distractions (family, pets, chores)
- Work-life boundary blur ("I'll just check Slack at 8 PM")
