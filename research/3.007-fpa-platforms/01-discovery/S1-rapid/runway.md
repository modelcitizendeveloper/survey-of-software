## 2. Core Modules

**Comprehensive FP&A Platform** (7/7 core modules):

✅ **Financial Modeling**
- Intuitive, human-readable formulas (not Excel-like complexity)
- Visual modeling interface (drag-and-drop blocks)
- Dynamic linking between assumptions and outcomes
- Customizable time periods (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Driver-based modeling (link metrics to financial outcomes)

✅ **Cash Flow Forecasting**
- 13-week cash flow projections
- Runway tracking (months until cash runs out)
- Burn rate analysis
- Cash waterfall visualization
- Scenario-based cash forecasting

✅ **Headcount Planning**
- Org chart builder (visual hierarchy)
- Role-based planning (title, salary, start date, department)
- Compensation bands and merit increases
- Benefits cost modeling
- Ramp-to-productivity curves
- Automatic sync with HRIS (Rippling, Gusto, BambooHR)

✅ **Departmental Budgeting**
- Budget templates by department
- Opex categorization (SaaS tools, contractors, marketing spend)
- Budget vs actuals variance analysis
- Approval workflows
- Version control and audit trails

✅ **Revenue Forecasting**
- ARR/MRR modeling for SaaS companies
- Pipeline-based forecasting (CRM integration)
- Churn and expansion revenue modeling
- Cohort analysis
- Unit economics

✅ **Scenario Planning**
- Unlimited scenarios (compare side-by-side)
- Scenario comparisons with AI-generated summaries
- Sensitivity analysis
- Real-time scenario switching
- Scenario archiving and versioning

✅ **Reporting & Dashboards**
- Customizable dashboards
- Board deck exports (PDF, PowerPoint)
- Variance analysis reports (budget vs actuals)
- KPI tracking
- Shareable links (stakeholder view-only access)

**Unique AI Features** (beyond traditional FP&A):

✅ **Ambient Intelligence** (launched July 2024)
- **Driver Explanations**: Auto-generated hover-over definitions for all financial terms
- **BvA Variance Analysis**: Automatic budget vs actuals variance detection and explanation
- **Scenario Summaries**: AI-written comparisons between scenarios
- **Invisible AI**: Works in background, no chat interface needed

✅ **Runway Copilot** (generative AI assistant)
- Generate scenarios from natural language ("What if revenue grows 50%?")
- Bulk formula creation (create 10 expense categories at once)
- Model suggestions based on company type
- Scenario generation in seconds (vs hours manually)

✅ **Automated Actuals**
- Auto-sync with accounting systems (QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite)
- Auto-update forecasts when actuals change
- Variance alerts when actuals deviate from forecast
- No manual import/export

## 5. Key Differentiators

### 1. **Ambient Intelligence (Industry First)**
Launched July 2024 - first FP&A platform with "invisible AI"

**What it is**:
- AI works in background, not chat interface
- Automatically explains financial terms on hover
- Auto-detects budget vs actual variances
- Summarizes scenario differences without prompting

**Why it matters**:
- Non-finance executives can understand financial models
- No AI prompt engineering needed (vs competitors requiring chat queries)
- Saves 5-10 hours/month explaining financial concepts to stakeholders

**Example**: Hover over "Gross Margin" → AI explains: "Revenue minus direct costs (COGS). Your gross margin is 75%, above SaaS industry average of 70%."

### 2. **Native HRIS Integration** (Rippling, Gusto, BambooHR)
Deep two-way sync with modern HRIS platforms

**What it does**:
- Pull employee data (salary, title, start date, department) automatically
- Auto-update headcount forecast when someone is hired in Rippling
- Push hiring plan back to HRIS (optional)

**Why it matters**:
- Solves "Planful doesn't integrate with Rippling" problem (from forum post)
- No manual employee data entry
- Headcount forecast always matches reality

**Comparison**:
- **Runway**: Native Rippling integration (1-click setup)
- **Planful**: No Rippling integration (requires custom middleware)
- **Anaplan**: Rippling via API only (manual mapping)

### 3. **Fast Time-to-Value** (1-2 weeks vs 3-6 months)
Designed for self-service implementation

**Setup timeline**:
- **Week 1**: Connect integrations (accounting, HRIS), import historical data
- **Week 2**: Build first budget model using templates, create 3 scenarios

**Comparison**:
- **Runway**: 1-2 weeks (self-service or guided)
- **Vena**: 4-8 weeks (requires consultant)
- **Planful**: 3-6 months (heavy implementation)
- **Anaplan**: 4-12 months (extremely complex)

**Why it's faster**:
- Pre-built templates for common company types (SaaS, ecommerce)
- Auto-sync with integrations (no manual data entry)
- Modern UX (intuitive vs legacy platforms requiring training)

### 4. **Collaborative Planning** (Built for Remote Teams)
Real-time multi-user editing (like Google Docs)

**Features**:
- Multiple users edit same model simultaneously
- In-line comments and @mentions
- Version history with restore
- Role-based permissions (viewer, editor, admin)
- Shareable links for stakeholders

**Why it matters**:
- Remote-first companies need async collaboration
- Department heads can own their budgets (not bottlenecked on finance team)
- No "Budget_v3_final_FINAL.xlsx" version control chaos

### 5. **Unlimited Scenarios**
Model as many futures as needed (vs 3-5 limit on competitors)

**Use cases**:
- Fundraising: Model Series B ($10M), Series B ($15M), Series B ($20M) scenarios
- Hiring: Model aggressive (30 hires), moderate (15 hires), freeze (0 hires)
- Revenue: Model optimistic (50% growth), base (30%), pessimistic (10%)

**Comparison**:
- **Runway**: Unlimited scenarios
- **Causal**: Unlimited scenarios
- **Adaptive Insights**: Unlimited scenarios
- **Some legacy tools**: 3-5 scenario limit

### 6. **Modern SaaS Metrics** (ARR, MRR, CAC, LTV)
Pre-built for SaaS companies (vs general-purpose tools)

**SaaS-specific features**:
- ARR/MRR waterfall (new, expansion, churn, contraction)
- Cohort analysis
- CAC payback period
- LTV:CAC ratio
- Magic number (sales efficiency)
- Rule of 40 tracking

**Why it matters**:
- SaaS companies are 60%+ of Runway's customer base
- Legacy platforms (Planful, Anaplan) require custom formulas for SaaS metrics
- Runway = out-of-box SaaS templates

### 7. **Browser-Based** (No Desktop Install)
100% cloud, works on any device

**Benefits**:
- No software installation
- Works on Mac, Windows, Chromebook
- Mobile-friendly (iPad, phone)
- Auto-updates (no manual version upgrades)

**Comparison**:
- **Runway**: 100% browser-based
- **Most competitors**: 100% browser-based (table stakes in 2025)
- **Excel**: Desktop-based, version control nightmare

## 7. Implementation & Onboarding

### Implementation Paths

**1. Self-Service** (most common for <100 employees)
- **Timeline**: 1-2 weeks
- **Cost**: $0-2K
- **Support**: Documentation, video tutorials, async chat
- **Who**: DIY finance team

**2. Guided Setup** (recommended for 100-500 employees)
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks
- **Cost**: $2K-5K
- **Support**: Dedicated onboarding specialist (5-10 hours)
- **Deliverables**: Integrations configured, first model built, team trained

**3. Custom Implementation** (complex scenarios)
- **Timeline**: 4-8 weeks
- **Cost**: $5K-10K
- **Support**: Implementation consultant
- **Use cases**: Multi-entity consolidation, custom integrations, migration from legacy tool

### Onboarding Steps

**Week 1: Foundations**
1. Connect accounting integration (QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite)
2. Import 2-3 years of historical actuals
3. Connect HRIS integration (Rippling, Gusto)
4. Map chart of accounts to expense categories

**Week 2: Model Building**
5. Choose template (SaaS, ecommerce, services)
6. Build headcount plan (import current employees, add hiring plan)
7. Create department budgets (marketing, engineering, sales, G&A)
8. Model revenue forecast (ARR, pipeline, or unit-based)

**Week 3: Scenarios & Reports**
9. Create 3 scenarios (optimistic, base, pessimistic)
10. Build dashboards for stakeholders
11. Train department heads on budget input
12. Set up approval workflows

**Week 4: Launch**
13. Review model with finance team
14. Present to leadership/board
15. Establish monthly update cadence

### Training Resources
- **Video tutorials**: 20+ hours of content
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive help center
- **Live onboarding calls**: 1-hour sessions
- **Office hours**: Weekly Q&A with customer success
- **Template library**: 10+ industry-specific templates

### Change Management
**Key challenges**:
- Department heads unfamiliar with FP&A tools (vs Excel)
- Finance team learning new platform
- Integrating into monthly close process

**Runway approach**:
- Consumer-grade UX (minimal training needed)

## 9. Platform Limitations

### ❌ Not Enterprise-Scale (2000+ employees)
Runway handles up to ~500 employees well. Beyond that, Planful/Anaplan better suited.

**Limitations**:
- Complex consolidation (10+ entities, multi-currency, intercompany)
- Advanced workflow approvals (multi-level, role-based routing)
- Supply chain planning (not Runway's focus)

### ❌ No Advanced Consolidation Features
Basic multi-entity roll-ups supported, but not:
- Intercompany eliminations (automatic)
- Multi-GAAP reporting (US GAAP vs IFRS)
- Currency translation (beyond basic FX rates)

**Workaround**: Use accounting system for consolidation, Runway for planning.

### ❌ Pricing Not Transparent (Custom Quotes)
No published pricing. Requires sales demo.

**Impact**: Cannot self-evaluate budget fit. Sales cycle adds 2-4 weeks.

### ❌ Newer Platform (Founded 2020, Launched 2024)
Less proven than 20-year-old competitors (Adaptive, Planful).

**Risks**:
- Feature gaps vs mature platforms
- Fewer customer references
- Potential for acquisition/pivot

**Mitigant**: Strong VC backing ($33.5M), 20x revenue growth, high customer satisfaction.

### ❌ Limited Customization (Opinionated Templates)
Runway provides templates = fast setup. But less flexible than blank-slate tools (Causal).

**Trade-off**:
- **Runway**: Opinionated templates, fast setup, less flexibility
- **Causal**: Blank slate, slow setup, infinite flexibility

### ❌ Integration Gaps (Bronze Tier)
650+ integrations sounds comprehensive, but "Bronze tier" = custom work.

**Not Gold/Silver**:
- Many mid-market ERP systems (Microsoft Dynamics, Epicor)
- Niche HRIS platforms (Paylocity, UKG)
- Legacy on-premise systems

**Impact**: Setup takes 2-4 weeks instead of 1-2 weeks.

### ❌ AI Hype Risk (Ambient Intelligence = New)
Ambient Intelligence launched July 2024. Too new to evaluate long-term value.

**Questions**:
- Will AI-generated explanations be accurate?
- Will variance detection replace analyst work or just augment?
- Will Copilot scenario generation be useful or gimmick?

**Wait-and-see**: 6-12 months of customer feedback needed.
