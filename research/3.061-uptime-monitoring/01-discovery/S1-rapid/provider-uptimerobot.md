### Free Tier Details

**Monitor Limits:**
- 50 monitors included (most generous free tier in market)
- Types: HTTP(s), Keyword, Ping, Port monitoring

**Check Intervals:**
- 5-minute intervals on free tier
- Sufficient for most small app monitoring needs

**Alerting:**
- Email alerts: Unlimited (free)
- SMS alerts: Pay-per-use (separate purchase)
- Phone call alerts: Pay-per-use

**Status Pages:**
- 1 public status page included (free)
- Custom domain support available
- Password protection option

**Data Retention:**
- 3 months of historical logs on free tier
- Limited compared to paid tiers (2 years)

**Monitoring Locations:**
- Multiple geographic regions
- Automatic rotation between locations
