# Better Uptime (Better Stack)
## Modern Developer-Focused Uptime Monitoring

### Market Position
- Part of Better Stack ecosystem (logs, uptime, incident management)
- Modern UI/UX designed for developers
- Growing popularity among startups and tech teams
- Not open source (proprietary SaaS)

### Free Tier Details

**Monitor Limits:**
- 10 monitors included
- Types: HTTP(s), TCP, Ping, Heartbeat (cron jobs)

**Check Intervals:**
- 3-minute intervals on free tier
- Fastest free tier interval among competitors
- Better than UptimeRobot's 5-minute free tier

**Alerting:**
- Email alerts: Unlimited (free)
- SMS alerts: Paid plans only
- Phone calls: Paid plans only
- Integrations: 1 integration on free (Slack, PagerDuty, etc.)
- Incident management built-in

**Status Pages:**
- 1 public status page included (free)
- Clean, modern design
- Automatic incident updates

**Team Features:**
- No team members on free tier (single user only)
- No SSL certificate monitoring on free tier

**Data Retention:**
- 30 days of uptime history
- Incident timeline included

**Monitoring Locations:**
- Global monitoring network
- Automatic geographic distribution

### Paid Tier Pricing

**Freelance Plan: $24/month**
- 50 monitors
- 30-second check intervals
- Unlimited email, SMS, phone alerts
- Unlimited team members
- SSL monitoring included

**Startup Plan: $49/month**
- 150 monitors
- All Freelance features
- On-call scheduling
- Escalation policies

**Business Plan: $99/month**
- 500 monitors
- Advanced incident management
- Custom integrations
- Priority support

### Key Differentiator
Best-in-class free tier check interval (3 minutes) combined with modern incident management UI makes it ideal for developer teams who value fast feedback loops.

### Best For
- Solo developers wanting faster alerts than UptimeRobot
- Startups planning to scale monitoring needs
- Teams already using Better Stack ecosystem
- Projects requiring clean status pages
