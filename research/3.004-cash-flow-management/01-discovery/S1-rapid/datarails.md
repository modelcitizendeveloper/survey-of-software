## 10. Cash Flow Specific Capabilities

### Cash Management Solution (Launched 2024)

**Background**:
- Dedicated cash management module launched 2024
- Purpose-built for CFOs managing cash flow
- Integrates with broader FP&A platform
- Positioned as "automation for cash flow forecasting"

### Cash Flow Forecasting

**Real-Time Cash Flow Models**:
- Automated data consolidation from ERP, billing, payroll systems
- Real-time updates (vs. manual Excel refresh)
- Cash inflows and outflows by category
- Rolling forecasts (13-week, monthly, quarterly)

**Direct Cash Flow Method**:
- Cash receipts from customers (AR-based)
- Cash payments to suppliers (AP-based)
- Payroll and employee cash outflows
- Operating, investing, financing cash flows

**Indirect Cash Flow Method**:
- P&L to cash flow reconciliation
- Non-cash adjustments (depreciation, amortization)
- Working capital changes

### Working Capital Management

**Current Assets Tracking**:
- Accounts receivable aging and DSO analysis
- Inventory turnover and cash tied up
- Cash and cash equivalents position

**Current Liabilities Tracking**:
- Accounts payable aging and DPO analysis
- Accrued expenses and short-term debt
- Payment schedule forecasting

**Cash Conversion Cycle**:
- DSO + DIO - DPO calculation
- Optimization scenarios for working capital
- Payment terms impact analysis

### Cash Runway Analysis

**Burn Rate Tracking**:
- Monthly, weekly, daily burn rate
- Gross burn vs. net burn
- Runway months until cash out (startup focus)

**Scenario-Based Cash Planning**:
- Best case, base case, worst case scenarios
- Hiring plan impact on cash runway
- Revenue growth scenarios and cash impact
- Funding round timing scenarios

### Multi-Currency Cash Flow

**Foreign Exchange Management**:
- Multi-currency consolidation
- FX rate assumptions and scenarios
- Currency exposure analysis

### Cash Flow Reporting & Drill-Down

**Drill-Down Capabilities** (unique strength):
- Drill down cash flow by account
- Department-level cash analysis
- Currency-specific cash positions
- Transaction-level detail (trace cash movements)

**Standard Reports**:
- Cash flow statement (operating, investing, financing)
- Cash waterfall visualization
- 13-week cash flow forecast
- Cash position dashboard

**Excel-Based Customization**:
- Custom cash flow reports in Excel
- Pivot table analysis of cash data
- Flexible formatting and presentation

### Integration with 3-Statement Model

**Automated Consolidation**:
- P&L, balance sheet, cash flow interconnected
- Changes to P&L automatically update cash flow
- Balance sheet changes flow to working capital cash impact
- Single source of truth in Excel

**Real-Time Actuals vs. Budget**:
- Actual cash flow from ERP/banking data
- Budget cash flow from forecast model
- Automated variance analysis
- Alert notifications for cash variances
