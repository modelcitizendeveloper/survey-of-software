## 2. Core Modules

**Cash Flow Focused Platform** (3/7 modules):

✅ **Cash Flow Forecasting**
- Multiple cash flow views (yearly, monthly, weekly, daily)
- Visual cash flow reports with charts
- Runway tracking (how long until cash runs out)
- Scenario testing ("what-if" analysis)
- Project-level cash flow tracking
- Customer-level cash flow analysis

✅ **Reporting & Dashboards**
- Visual cash flow reports
- Customer and project-level reporting
- Multi-currency support
- Customizable views by time period

✅ **Collaboration**
- Team collaboration with access controls
- Multi-user support
- Permission management

❌ **NOT Included**:
- Full FP&A (budgeting, forecasting beyond cash)
- Headcount planning
- Departmental budgeting
- Revenue forecasting (beyond cash flow)
