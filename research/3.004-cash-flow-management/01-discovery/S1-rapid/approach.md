# S1 Rapid Discovery - Approach

**Experiment**: 3.004 Cash Flow Management
**Stage**: S1 - Rapid Discovery
**Date**: October 22, 2025
**Updated**: November 13, 2025 (migrated to detailed_toc format, adding Pigment and Datarails)

---

## Methodology

**Goal**: Establish baseline understanding of cash flow and FP&A platform landscape through quick provider assessments.

**Scope**: 10 platforms across 3 market tiers (Small Business, Startup/SMB, Mid-Market/Enterprise)

**Time Investment**: 30-60 minutes per platform (5-10 hours total)

**Output**: Individual platform profiles capturing:
- Company overview, founding, funding, scale
- Core modules and capabilities (cash flow specific)
- Target market (company size, industry, use cases)
- Pricing model and cost structure
- Key differentiators
- Notable customers
- Integration capabilities
- Platform strengths and limitations

---

## Platform Selection Criteria

**Tier 1: Small Business Cash Flow** ($29-100/month)
- Target: 1-20 employees
- Setup: 1-3 days
- Use case: Cash visibility, simple forecasting

**Tier 2: Startup/SMB FP&A** ($100-500/month)
- Target: 20-100 employees
- Setup: 1-2 weeks
- Use case: Financial modeling, scenario planning, headcount planning

**Tier 3: Mid-Market to Enterprise** ($500-2,000+/month, up to $100K+/year)
- Target: 50-500+ employees
- Setup: 2-8 weeks
- Use case: Strategic finance, consolidation, advanced FP&A

## Data Sources

**Primary Sources**:
- Platform websites (public marketing, pricing pages where available)
- Technical documentation (API docs, integration guides)
- Platform blog posts, case studies, product announcements
- Integration marketplace data
- Recent funding announcements, acquisition news

**Secondary Sources**:
- Industry analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester)
- User review aggregates (G2, Capterra - public summary data only)
- Procurement benchmarking (Vendr, TrustRadius)

**Limitations**:
- Pricing: 8 of 10 platforms do not publish transparent pricing (custom quotes)
- Customer counts: Not all platforms disclose
- Implementation costs: Estimates based on industry benchmarks
- Churn rates: Not publicly available

---

## Research Philosophy

**Hardware Store Principle**: Generic catalog content applicable to any buyer context. No client-specific recommendations or "Choose X if..." prescriptive guidance in S1.

**Factual Focus**: Document what platforms provide, not which to choose.

**Integration Emphasis**: Capture accounting, ERP, HRIS, CRM, data warehouse integration capabilities (critical for cash flow/FP&A value).

**Cost Transparency**: Document pricing where available, estimate TCO where not published.

**Cash Flow Focus**: While many platforms are full FP&A suites, emphasize cash flow forecasting, cash runway tracking, and working capital management capabilities.
