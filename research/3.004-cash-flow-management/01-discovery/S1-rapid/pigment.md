## 10. Cash Flow Specific Capabilities

### Cash Flow Forecasting

**Direct Cash Flow Method**:
- Cash receipts from customers
- Cash payments to suppliers and employees
- Operating, investing, and financing cash flows
- Granular cash flow by department, region, product

**Indirect Cash Flow Method**:
- Derived from P&L and balance sheet
- Non-cash adjustments (depreciation, amortization)
- Working capital changes

### Working Capital Management

**Current Assets Optimization**:
- Accounts receivable forecasting (DSO analysis)
- Inventory turnover modeling
- Cash and cash equivalents planning

**Current Liabilities Optimization**:
- Accounts payable forecasting (DPO analysis)
- Accrued expenses planning
- Short-term debt management

**Cash Conversion Cycle**:
- DSO + DIO - DPO calculation
- Scenario modeling for working capital optimization
- Impact analysis of payment terms changes

### Cash Runway Analysis

**Burn Rate Tracking**:
- Monthly, weekly, daily burn rate
- Gross burn vs. net burn
- Runway months until cash out

**Scenario-Based Runway**:
- Best case, base case, worst case cash scenarios
- Hiring plan impact on runway
- Revenue growth scenarios and cash impact

### Multi-Currency Cash Flow

**Foreign Exchange Management**:
- Multi-currency cash flow consolidation
- FX rate assumptions and scenarios
- Currency exposure analysis

### Cash Flow Reporting

**Standard Reports**:
- Cash flow statement (operating, investing, financing)
- Cash waterfall visualization
- Rolling 13-week cash flow forecast
- Cash position dashboard

**Custom Reports**:
- Self-service report builder
- Department/region/product cash flow analysis
- Project-level cash flow tracking

### Integration with 3-Statement Model

**Unified Planning**:
- P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow interconnected
- Changes to P&L automatically flow to cash flow
- Balance sheet changes update working capital cash impact
- Single source of truth for financial planning
