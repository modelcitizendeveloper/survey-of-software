# S4: Strategic Selection - Approach

**Goal:** Provide strategic guidance for long-term accounting software decisions

**Time Budget:** 1-2 hours

**Methodology:**
1. Vendor viability analysis (financial stability, acquisition risk)
2. Strategic decision frameworks (when to choose which platform)
3. Graduation triggers (when to upgrade/migrate)
4. Lock-in and migration strategies
5. Future-proofing recommendations

---

## Key Strategic Questions

### Vendor Viability
- Which vendors are financially stable long-term?
- What's the acquisition risk for each platform?
- Which platforms have 10+ year track records?

### Strategic Fit
- How do you choose between accounting software vs ERP?
- When should you graduate from one tier to the next?
- What are the signals you've outgrown your current platform?

### Lock-In & Exit Strategy
- Which platforms have the highest vendor lock-in?
- How difficult is migration away from each platform?
- What's the cost of switching platforms?

### Future-Proofing
- Which platforms are investing in innovation (AI, automation)?
- Which platforms scale with business growth?
- Which platforms have the best long-term roadmaps?
