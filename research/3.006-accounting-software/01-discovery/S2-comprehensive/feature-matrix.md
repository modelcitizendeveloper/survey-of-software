## Key Insights

### Feature Leaders by Category

**Best Free Option:** Wave (core accounting at no cost)
**Best Small Business (Overall):** QuickBooks Online (most complete feature set)
**Best UX:** Xero (modern interface, mobile app)
**Best for Services:** FreshBooks (time tracking, project management)
**Best Budget Paid:** Zoho Books (features for price)
**Best Mid-Market Accounting:** Sage Intacct (dimensional reporting, consolidation)
**Best Full ERP:** NetSuite (comprehensive platform)
**Best Microsoft Integration:** Dynamics 365 (Power Platform, Office 365)

### Feature Gaps by Platform

**Wave:** No inventory, limited automation (free), no multi-entity
**Zoho Books:** Feature tiers lock capabilities, smaller ecosystem
**FreshBooks:** No inventory, limited financial reporting depth
**QuickBooks:** Price creep, complexity, frequent upselling
**Xero:** Inventory less robust, invoice limits on lower tiers
**Sage Intacct:** Expensive for small business, not full ERP
**NetSuite:** Overkill for accounting-only needs, expensive
**Dynamics 365:** Microsoft lock-in, accounting depth vs Sage Intacct
