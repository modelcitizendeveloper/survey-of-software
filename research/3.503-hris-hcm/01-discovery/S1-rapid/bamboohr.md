## 4. Pricing Model

**Structure**: Tiered plans, PEPM pricing (not transparently published)

### **Essentials Plan**
- **Price**: ~$250/month flat fee for ≤25 employees, then ~$12-15/employee/month for >25
- **Includes**:
  - Core HR (employee records, org chart, documents)
  - Basic reporting
  - Time-off tracking
  - Employee self-service
  - Mobile apps
  - Basic hiring (job posting, basic ATS)
  - Onboarding workflows
  - eSignatures
- **Best for**: 25-100 employees, basic HR needs

### **Advantage Plan** (Mid-Tier)
- **Price**: ~$15-18/employee/month (estimate)
- **Includes** (everything in Essentials, plus):
  - Custom workflows
  - Advanced reporting
  - Custom fields
  - Offboarding workflows
  - More integrations
- **Best for**: 100-250 employees, need customization

### **Pro Plan** (Most Popular)
- **Price**: ~$18-22/employee/month (estimate)
- **Includes** (everything in Advantage, plus):
  - Full ATS (interview scheduling, scorecards, pipeline)
  - Performance management
  - Employee satisfaction surveys
  - Advanced analytics
  - API access
  - Premium integrations
- **Best for**: 100-500 employees, active hiring, need performance mgmt

### **Add-Ons** (Additional Cost):
- **BambooHR Payroll**: +$6-10/employee/month (U.S. only)
- **Time Tracking**: +$4-6/employee/month
- **Performance Management**: Included in Pro, $5-8/employee/month if added to lower tiers
- **Employee Satisfaction surveys**: Included in Pro, $2-4/employee/month if added

### **Example Pricing** (100-employee company, Pro Plan):
- Base: 100 × $20/employee/month = $2,000/month = $24,000/year
- **With add-ons** (Payroll + Time Tracking):
  - HR: $20/employee/month
  - Payroll: $8/employee/month
  - Time: $5/employee/month
  - **Total**: ~$33/employee/month = $3,300/month = $39,600/year

**Implementation Fees**: $0-5,000 depending on complexity (data migration, integrations)

**Contract Terms**:
- Month-to-month (no annual contract required - unique for HRIS at this scale)
- Can cancel anytime
- Volume discounts (pricing decreases as employee count increases)

**Pricing Transparency**: ⭐⭐ Limited
- Not publicly listed (requires sales call)
- Estimates available on comparison sites ($12-22/employee/month range)
- More transparent than enterprise (Workday, Oracle), less than Gusto

## 8. Implementation

**Typical Timeline**:
- **Basic setup**: 1-2 weeks (employee data, org structure)
- **HR go-live**: 2-3 weeks (documents, workflows)
- **Payroll integration**: 3-4 weeks (if integrating with Gusto/ADP)
- **Full implementation** (HR + Payroll + Performance + Hiring): 4-8 weeks
- **Add-ons** (Time Tracking, Performance): Additional 1-2 weeks each

**Complexity**: Low to Moderate

**Implementation Process**:
1. **Kickoff call**: Customer success team (30-60 min)
2. **Data migration**: Import employee data (CSV or manual)
   - Historical data: Can import, but most start fresh
3. **Configuration**:
   - Custom fields (job titles, departments, locations)
   - Workflows (onboarding, offboarding, comp changes)
   - Approval chains (who approves PTO, hires, etc.)
4. **Payroll integration** (if not using BambooHR Payroll):
   - Connect to Gusto/ADP/Paylocity
   - Map fields (BambooHR fields → Payroll system)
   - Test sync (ensure employee data flows correctly)
5. **Employee rollout**:
   - Invite employees to self-onboard (fill profiles, upload docs)
   - Training session (optional live or self-paced videos)
6. **Go live**: Switch from old system to BambooHR
7. **Add-ons**: Enable Performance, Time Tracking, Hiring as needed

**Self-Service vs Consultant**:
- **Self-service**: Yes, most customers (especially <100 employees) self-implement
- **Consultant**: Available for complex migrations (especially 250+ employees, legacy systems)
- **Customer success**: Included, onboarding specialist assigned (more support with Pro plan)

**Data Migration**:
- **Employee data**: CSV import or API
- **Historical data**: Employment history, comp changes (can import or manual entry)
- **Documents**: Upload existing documents (I-9s, W-4s, reviews) to employee profiles
- **Reviews/performance**: Usually start fresh (historical reviews rarely migrated)

**Payroll Integration Cutover**:
- If using BambooHR Payroll (native): Standard payroll cutover (parallel runs)
- If integrating with Gusto/ADP: Employee data syncs, no "cutover" needed
- Lower risk than Rippling/Gusto (BambooHR doesn't run payroll directly)

**Implementation Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Very Good
- Moderate timeline (4-8 weeks typical)
- Self-service capable for SMBs
- Customer success support included
- Lower risk (HR system, not payroll)

## 10. Strengths & Weaknesses

### Strengths

**1. Best-of-Breed HR**
- Core HR features are excellent (employee records, org chart, workflows)
- Strong performance management (rivals Lattice, 15Five at lower cost)
- Full ATS (Pro plan) rivals Greenhouse, Lever
- Focus on HR depth vs trying to do everything (Rippling)

**2. Payroll Flexibility**
- Can use BambooHR Payroll (native) OR Gusto/ADP/Paylocity
- Best-of-breed approach: Choose best payroll for your needs
- Most common: BambooHR + Gusto (powerful combo)
- Freedom to switch payroll without changing HR system

**3. Exceptional User Experience**
- Visual, intuitive interface (org chart, directory, calendars)
- Clean design (feels modern vs "enterprise software")
- Mobile apps highly rated (4.6/5 stars)
- Employees actually enjoy using it

**4. No Annual Contracts**
- Month-to-month pricing (rare for HRIS at 100+ employees)
- Can cancel anytime (low switching cost)
- De-risks implementation

**5. Strong Performance Management**
- 360-degree reviews, goal cascading, continuous feedback
- Compensation review integration
- Better than Gusto (basic), competitive with Lattice (expensive)
- Often cited as reason to choose BambooHR

**6. Robust ATS (Pro Plan)**
- Full applicant tracking system
- Kanban pipeline, interview scheduling, scorecards
- Seamless hire-to-employee transition
- Rivals dedicated ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever) at lower cost

**7. Implementation Support**
- Customer success team assigned
- BambooHR University (free training)
- Strong onboarding process
- 4-8 week typical implementation (reasonable)

### Weaknesses

**1. Payroll Module is Add-On**
- Unlike Gusto (payroll core), BambooHR Payroll is optional
- Most customers integrate with Gusto/ADP (adds complexity)
- BambooHR Payroll (native) is U.S. only, not as mature as Gusto's
- Integration overhead: Syncing data between BambooHR ↔ Gusto

**2. No IT Management**
- HR-only (unlike Rippling's HR + IT + Finance)
- No device management, SSO, app provisioning
- Tech companies need separate MDM (Jamf, Kandji)

**3. Limited Global Capabilities**
- BambooHR Payroll U.S. only
- No global payroll (unlike Rippling, Deel, Remote)
- Can integrate global payroll, but not native

**4. No Learning Management**
- No LMS module (training, compliance, certifications)
- Must integrate TalentLMS, Lessonly, others
- Competitors (Rippling, Workday) include LMS

**5. Pricing Not Transparent**
- Requires sales call for quote (unlike Gusto's public pricing)
- Estimates: $12-22/employee/month, but varies
- Add-ons can increase cost ($30-40/employee/month with all features)

**6. Limited at 500+ Employees**
- Sweet spot is 25-500 employees
- At 500+, customers outgrow BambooHR
- No workforce analytics, succession planning (vs Workday, Oracle)
- Churn increases at 1000+ employees

**7. Benefits Administration Not as Deep**
- Benefits tracking and enrollment workflows good
- But not as comprehensive as Gusto (which brokers insurance)
- No carrier markup-free partnerships (Gusto provides licensed brokers)
- Fine for companies with existing benefits broker

### Best For

**BambooHR is BEST for**:
- SMBs (50-250 employees) with dedicated HR person
- Companies wanting strong HR + flexible payroll choice
- Businesses needing performance management + ATS (Pro plan)
- Professional services, tech, healthcare, non-profits
- Teams valuing user experience and visual design
- Companies wanting month-to-month contracts (low commitment)

**Consider Alternatives If**:
- Under 25 employees (Gusto simpler and cheaper)
- Over 500 employees (Paycom, Workday, Oracle scale better)
- Need all-in-one (HR+IT+Finance) - Rippling better fit
- Payroll is primary need - Gusto better (payroll-first)
- Need global payroll - Rippling, Deel, Remote have international
- Need LMS - Rippling, Workday include learning management
- Want transparent pricing - Gusto publishes pricing
