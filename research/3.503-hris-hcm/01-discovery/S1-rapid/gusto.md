## 3. Target Market

**Ideal Customer**:
- **Employee count**: 2-100 employees (sweet spot: 10-50)
- **Company type**: Small businesses, startups, professional services
- **Profile**: Want simple payroll + benefits, not complex HR features
- **Values**: Ease of use, affordability, self-service

**Sweet Spot**:
- Small businesses (10-50 employees) needing payroll + basic HR
- Startups (seed to Series A) outgrowing manual payroll
- Service businesses (consulting, agencies, law firms)
- Retail, restaurants (hourly workers, time tracking)
- Single or multi-state operations (within U.S. only)

**Not For**:
- Businesses under 2 employees (contractor-only plan available, but not main focus)
- Enterprises (150+ employees) - complexity outgrows Gusto
- International companies - no global payroll (U.S. only)
- Companies needing deep performance management (Workday, Lattice better)
- Manufacturing with complex union rules (ADP better)

**Customer Demographics**:
- 300,000+ businesses (as of 2023)
- Median customer: 10-25 employees
- 80%+ have fewer than 100 employees
- Churn increases significantly above 150 employees (outgrow platform)

## 4. Pricing Model

**Structure**: Tiered plans with PEPM pricing

### **Simple Plan**
- **Price**: $40/month base + $6/employee/month
- **Includes**:
  - Full-service payroll
  - Tax filing (federal, state, local)
  - Employee self-onboarding
  - Direct deposit
  - Basic HR tools
- **Best for**: 1-10 employees, single-state

### **Plus Plan**
- **Price**: $80/month base + $12/employee/month
- **Includes** (everything in Simple, plus):
  - Multi-state payroll
  - Time tracking
  - PTO management
  - Custom reports
  - Workforce costing
  - Hiring and onboarding
  - Performance management (basic)
  - Projects and project tracking
- **Best for**: 10-50 employees, multi-state, need HR features

### **Premium Plan**
- **Price**: Custom quote (est. $150-200/month base + $20-25/employee/month)
- **Includes** (everything in Plus, plus):
  - Dedicated customer success manager
  - Priority support (faster response)
  - Certified HR experts access
  - Compliance alerts (custom to your business)
  - Admin migration assistance
  - Custom onboarding
  - Scalability consultation
- **Best for**: 50-100+ employees, complex needs

### **Contractor-Only Plan**
- **Price**: $6/contractor/month (no base fee for first 6 contractors, then $40/month base)
- **Includes**: 1099 contractor payments, tax forms
- **Best for**: Freelancers, agencies with contractors only

### **Example Pricing** (50-employee company, Plus Plan):

**Implementation Fees**: $0 (no setup fee)

**Contract Terms**:

**Pricing Transparency**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent

- Payroll is core strength (not an afterthought like BambooHR)
- "Set and forget" automated payroll (runs on schedule without admin action)
- Accuracy guarantee: Gusto pays penalties if they make payroll tax error

### 2. **Transparent, Affordable Pricing**
- Only major HRIS with publicly listed pricing
- No sales calls required (instant signup)
- Month-to-month contracts (no lock-in)

## 8. Implementation

**Typical Timeline**:
- **Setup account**: 10 minutes (signup online, no sales call)
- **Add employees**: 30 minutes (employees self-onboard)
- **Payroll setup**: 30-60 minutes (tax info, pay schedules, deductions)
- **First payroll**: 1-2 weeks (verify everything correct before first run)
- **Benefits setup**: 1-2 weeks (if adding health insurance, 401(k))
- **Full implementation**: 2-4 weeks (from signup to first payroll)

**Complexity**: Very Low (DIY, no consultant needed)

**Implementation Process**:
1. **Sign up online**: Instant access (no sales call)
2. **Company setup**: EIN, address, bank account, pay schedule
3. **Employee setup**: Invite employees, they self-onboard (enter SSN, tax info, bank)
4. **Payroll setup**: Review employees, verify tax withholding
5. **Test payroll**: Optional test run (no money moves)
6. **Go live**: Run first real payroll
7. **Benefits setup**: (Optional) Add health insurance, 401(k), workers' comp

**Self-Service vs Consultant**:
- **Self-service**: Yes, 95%+ of customers self-implement
- **Consultant needed**: No (Gusto designed for DIY)
- **Migration assistance**: Premium plan includes dedicated CSM help
- **Onboarding calls**: Optional (offered but not required)

**Data Migration**:
- **Employee data**: CSV import or manual entry (self-onboarding easier)
- **Historical payroll**: YTD payroll data can be imported (for mid-year switches)
- **Benefits**: Re-enrollment required (can't import elections from old system)
- **Note**: Most customers start fresh (minimal historical data needed for payroll)

**Payroll Cutover**:
- **Parallel runs**: Recommended (run Gusto alongside old system for 1 pay cycle)
- **Validation**: Compare paychecks to ensure accuracy
- **Cutover**: Switch after successful parallel run
- **Risk**: Low (Gusto has accuracy guarantee, will fix errors)

**Implementation Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent
- Fastest implementation among HRIS platforms
- True self-service (no consultant required)
- 10-minute signup to first payroll in 2-4 weeks
- Minimal disruption

## 10. Strengths & Weaknesses

### Strengths

**1. Unmatched Ease of Use**
- Simplest HRIS on market (consistently rated #1 for ease of use)
- 10-minute signup, 2-4 week implementation
- Anyone can run payroll without training
- Consumer-grade UI (feels like using Stripe or Square)

**2. Transparent, Affordable Pricing**
- Only HRIS with publicly listed pricing
- No sales calls, no contracts (month-to-month)
- Low cost: $40-80/month base (competitors: $150-300/month)
- No hidden fees

**3. Payroll Excellence**
- Core competency (started as ZenPayroll)
- Automated tax filing (federal, state, local)
- Multi-state compliance (all 50 states)
- Accuracy guarantee (Gusto pays penalties if they err)
- Same-day payroll (emergency payments)

**4. Self-Service Everything**
- Employees onboard themselves
- Managers approve PTO, time
- Admins run payroll in 3 clicks
- Minimal HR involvement needed

**5. Benefits Made Easy**
- Free licensed insurance brokers
- 80+ carrier integrations
- 401(k) integration (Guideline seamless)
- Workers' comp built-in

**6. API Ecosystem**
- First modern payroll API (2013)
- 200+ integrations
- Gusto Embedded (white-label payroll for vertical SaaS)
- Developer-friendly documentation

**7. Fast Implementation**
- No consultant required (true DIY)
- First payroll in 2-4 weeks
- Minimal disruption
- CSV import or employee self-onboarding

### Weaknesses

**1. Limited to U.S. Only**
- No global payroll (unlike Rippling, Deel, Remote)
- Can't pay international employees or contractors
- Workaround: Use Deel/Remote alongside Gusto (but adds complexity)

**2. Weak Performance Management**
- Basic goal setting and feedback
- No 360-degree reviews
- No calibration sessions
- No succession planning
- Startups needing performance tools use Lattice/15Five alongside Gusto

**3. No Learning Management**
- No training/LMS module
- Must integrate third-party LMS (Lessonly, TalentLMS)
- Competitors (Rippling, Workday) include LMS

**4. Limited at 100+ Employees**
- Sweet spot is 10-50 employees
- At 100+, customers outgrow Gusto
- No workforce analytics (vs Workday, Oracle)
- No advanced compensation planning
- Churn increases at 150+ employees

**5. No IT Management**
- Payroll/HR only (unlike Rippling's HR + IT + Finance)
- No device management, SSO, app provisioning
- Tech companies still need separate MDM (Jamf, Kandji)

**6. Basic Reporting**
- Standard reports good, custom reports available (Plus plan)
- But not as powerful as enterprise platforms (Workday dashboards)
- No workforce planning, predictive analytics

**7. Implementation Support Variability**
- Premium plan gets white-glove, lower tiers get self-service
- Simple/Plus customers may feel unsupported during complex migrations
- No dedicated onboarding specialist unless Premium

### Best For

**Gusto is BEST for**:
- Small businesses (10-50 employees) needing simple payroll + HR
- U.S.-only operations (all employees domestic)
- Companies wanting transparent pricing (no sales calls)
- Businesses needing fast setup (go live in 2-4 weeks)
- Self-service preference (don't want consultant dependency)
- Budget-conscious (keep costs low)
- Hourly workforce (retail, hospitality, construction)

**Consider Alternatives If**:
- Over 100 employees (Paycom, ADP Workforce Now scale better)
- Need global payroll (Rippling, Deel, Remote have international)
- Need IT management (Rippling includes device mgmt, SSO)
- Need deep performance management (Lattice, Workday stronger)
- Need LMS (training/compliance) - Rippling, Workday include it
- Enterprise features (succession planning, workforce analytics) - Workday, Oracle
- Prefer white-glove support - ADP Workforce Now, Paycom

## Competitive Positioning

### vs Rippling
- **Gusto**: Simpler, cheaper, payroll-first, U.S.-only, no IT mgmt
- **Rippling**: More comprehensive (HR + IT + Finance), global, more expensive, steeper learning curve
- **When Gusto wins**: 10-50 employees, U.S.-only, budget-conscious, want simplicity
- **When Rippling wins**: Need IT management, global payroll, 50-200 employees, can afford $50-70/employee/month

### vs BambooHR
- **Gusto**: Includes payroll (core product), simpler, cheaper
- **BambooHR**: HR-only (integrates with Gusto or others for payroll), stronger performance management, more expensive
- **When Gusto wins**: Want all-in-one (payroll + HR), budget <$50/employee/month
- **When BambooHR wins**: Want best-of-breed HR tool, already have payroll solution, need deeper HR features

### vs ADP Workforce Now
- **Gusto**: Modern UI, transparent pricing, fast setup, self-service
- **ADP**: Established, more features, white-glove support, traditional industries
- **When Gusto wins**: Tech-forward companies, want modern UI, <100 employees
- **When ADP wins**: >100 employees, traditional industry (manufacturing), want dedicated support

### vs Paycom
- **Gusto**: Simpler, cheaper, faster setup, better for SMB
- **Paycom**: More comprehensive, single database, employee self-service (Beti), better for 250+ employees
- **When Gusto wins**: <100 employees, want simplicity, budget-conscious
- **When Paycom wins**: 250-2000 employees, want employee-driven payroll, need deep analytics

**Gusto's Niche**: The "Stripe of payroll" - simple, transparent, developer-friendly HRIS for SMBs (10-100 employees) focused on U.S. payroll + basic HR.
