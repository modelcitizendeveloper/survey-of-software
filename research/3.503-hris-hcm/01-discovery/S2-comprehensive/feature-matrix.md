## Feature Gap Analysis

### What No Platform Does Well (Market Gaps)

1. **True Global Payroll** (50+ countries natively)
   - Best option: ADP GlobalView (payroll aggregator, not HRIS)
   - HRIS platforms partner with CloudPay, Safeguard Global, local bureaus

2. **Employee Financial Wellness** (beyond 401k)
   - Gusto offers 401k, student loan repayment
   - Most platforms integrate with Northstar, SmartDollar, but not native

3. **Gig/Contractor Management at Scale**
   - Rippling best for global contractors (185 countries)
   - Enterprise platforms treat contractors as second-class (Workday/Oracle focus on FTE)

4. **Frontline/Deskless Worker Experience**
   - Paylocity Community attempts this
   - Most platforms assume desk workers with computers
   - Specialized: WorkJam, Beekeeper, Workplace from Meta

5. **DEI Analytics & Bias Detection**
   - Workday/Oracle have pay equity analysis
   - None have AI bias detection in hiring, performance reviews
   - Third-party: Textio, Applied, GapJumpers
