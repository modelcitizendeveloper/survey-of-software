# Provider: Honeybadger

## Overview
Honeybadger is an all-in-one monitoring solution combining error tracking, uptime monitoring, and cron/scheduled job monitoring. Known for transparent pricing and developer-friendly policies.

## Popularity
- **GitHub Presence**: Open-source client libraries (honeybadger-ruby, etc.)
- **Licensing**: MIT licensed SDKs
- **Market Position**: Recognized as simpler, lightweight alternative to enterprise APM

## Pricing (2025)
**Model**: Fixed monthly subscription (predictable costs)

**Free Developer Plan**:
- 5,000 errors/month
- 15 days error retention
- 50 MB/day log storage
- Basic uptime and check-in monitoring

**Team Plan**:
- $39/month starting price
- Unlimited users (no per-seat charges)
- No overage fees (they absorb excess usage)
- Transparent, forgiving pricing model

**Higher Tiers**:
- Team 150k, 600k, 1.8M, 3.6M
- Business plans available

## Setup Time
**Estimated**: 5-10 minutes
- Simple SDK installation
- Straightforward configuration
- Minimal setup for error capture

## Key Differentiators
1. **All-in-one monitoring** - Errors + uptime + cron monitoring in one package
2. **Transparent pricing** - Fixed costs, no surprise bills or overage charges
3. **Unlimited users** - No per-seat pricing
4. **Self-hosting option** - Complete data privacy and control
5. **Developer-friendly policies** - Forgiving limits, absorbs accidental overages

## Best For
- Small to medium teams wanting predictable costs
- Projects needing unified error and uptime monitoring
- Teams prioritizing pricing simplicity and transparency
- Organizations requiring self-hosting options
