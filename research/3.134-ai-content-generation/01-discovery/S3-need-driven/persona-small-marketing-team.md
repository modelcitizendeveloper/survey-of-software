# Persona: Small Marketing Team (2-5 People)

**Research Phase**: S3 Need-Driven Discovery (MPSE v3.0)
**Date**: November 2025

---

## Persona Profile

### Demographics
- **Company Size**: 10-50 employees (small to mid-size business)
- **Team Structure**:
  - 1 Marketing Manager/Director (team lead)
  - 1-2 Content Creators/Writers
  - 1 Designer (often shared with other departments)
  - 0-1 Social Media Manager (may be combined role)
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS, professional services, e-commerce, healthcare, fintech
- **Budget**: $50-200K annual marketing budget
- **Content Volume**: 50-100 pieces per month across all channels

### Pain Points
- **Capacity Constraints**: Too much content needed, too few people
- **Approval Bottlenecks**: Manager must review everything, slows down publishing
- **Inconsistent Quality**: Quality varies by who writes (skill level differences)
- **Brand Voice Drift**: Different team members write differently, lacks cohesion
- **Platform Overload**: Need content for LinkedIn, Twitter, email, blog, ads, sales collateral
- **Workflow Chaos**: No standardized process, recreating templates constantly
- **Turnover Risk**: Losing a content person creates crisis (no documentation or systems)

### Current Content Workflow (Pre-AI)
1. Weekly content planning meeting (1-2 hours)
2. Assign content to team members
3. Each person writes their assigned pieces (10-20 hours/week total team time)
4. Submit for manager review and approval (2-4 hours manager time)
5. Edit and revise (3-5 hours)
6. Publish and schedule
7. **Result**: 40-60 pieces per month at 60-80 hours team effort

**Team Time Investment**: 60-80 hours/month
**Output**: 40-60 pieces per month
**Cost per Piece**: 1-1.5 hours of team time

## Collaboration Requirements

### Approval Workflows
- **Draft → Review → Revise → Approve → Publish**
- **Bottleneck**: Manager review takes 24-48 hours
- **Need**: Faster review cycle, clearer standards

### Brand Consistency
- **Challenge**: 3-5 different people writing = 3-5 different voices
- **Current Solution**: Brand guidelines (often ignored or misinterpreted)
- **Desired State**: Unified brand voice regardless of who creates content

### Task Management
- **Current Tools**: Asana, Monday.com, Trello, or spreadsheets
- **Pain Point**: Content creation separate from project management
- **Ideal**: Integrated workflow (task → generate content → review → publish)

### Knowledge Sharing
- **Problem**: "How did Sarah write that great LinkedIn post last month?"
- **Current**: No template library, recreate from scratch every time
- **Desired**: Shared template library, reusable prompts, best practices documentation

## Tool Selection Criteria

### Must-Haves
1. **Multi-user support**: 3-5 seats minimum
2. **Collaboration features**: Comments, approvals, version history
3. **Brand voice consistency**: Unified voice across all team members
4. **Template library**: Shared templates accessible to entire team
5. **Workflow integration**: Works with existing tools (Asana, Slack, etc.)
6. **Quality control**: Manager can review before publishing

### Nice-to-Haves
1. **Content calendar integration**: Schedule and plan content
2. **Analytics**: Track performance of AI-generated vs manual content
3. **Role-based permissions**: Manager approves, team creates
4. **API access**: Custom integrations with CRM, CMS, etc.
5. **Bulk generation**: Create 20-30 posts at once

### Deal-Breakers
1. **No team plans**: Solo-only tools won't work
2. **Per-word pricing**: Causes anxiety for high-volume teams
3. **No approval workflow**: Can't publish without review
4. **Poor quality**: If output needs 30+ min editing, not worth it
5. **Steep learning curve**: Team won't adopt if complex

## Implementation Workflow

### Phase 1: Pilot Program (Month 1)

**Objective**: Test AI content generation with subset of team

**Steps**:
1. **Select pilot group** (Manager + 2 content creators)
2. **Choose tool**: Jasper Pro trial or Copy.ai Growth trial
3. **Create brand voice profile**:
   - Upload 10-15 best-performing content pieces
   - Document brand guidelines and tone
   - Train team on voice consistency
4. **Generate test content**:
   - 20 social posts
   - 5 blog posts
   - 10 email variations
   - 10 ad copy variations
5. **Measure results**:
   - Time saved vs manual creation
   - Quality assessment (1-10 scale)
   - Team feedback on ease of use
6. **Decision point**: Expand to full team or try alternative tool

**Success Metrics**:
- 40%+ time savings per content piece
- Quality rating 7+/10 with editing
- Positive team feedback (would recommend to colleagues)

### Phase 2: Team Rollout (Month 2-3)

**Objective**: Scale AI content across entire team

**Steps**:
1. **Onboard remaining team members**:
   - 1-hour training session per person
   - Create individual accounts/seats
   - Assign roles (creator, reviewer, admin)
2. **Build template library**:
   - 10-15 templates for common content types
   - Shared prompts and best practices
   - Document workflow processes
3. **Establish approval workflow**:
   - Draft content in AI tool
   - Submit for manager review (in-tool or via Slack/Asana)
   - Manager approves or requests revisions
   - Publish to channels
4. **Integrate with existing tools**:
   - Connect to Buffer/Hootsuite (social scheduling)
   - Link to Google Docs (collaborative editing)
   - Zapier automation (AI generation → task management)
5. **Set team goals**:
   - Increase output by 40-60% (40-60 pieces → 60-100 pieces)
   - Maintain or improve quality (7-8/10 rating)
   - Save 20-30 hours team time per month

**Success Metrics**:
- All team members actively using tool (3+ times/week)
- Output increase: 50%+ vs pre-AI baseline
- Time savings: 20-30 hours/month team total
- Manager review time: Reduced by 30-40% (better first drafts)

### Phase 3: Optimization (Month 4-6)

**Objective**: Maximize ROI and refine workflows

**Steps**:
1. **Analyze performance data**:
   - Which content types benefit most from AI?
   - Where is editing time still high? (indicates poor prompts)
   - What's the engagement of AI vs manual content?
2. **Refine brand voice**:
   - Update voice profile with new high-performing content
   - Create voice variations (formal, casual, technical)
3. **Expand use cases**:
   - Sales team: Proposal templates, email outreach
   - Customer success: Help center articles, onboarding emails
   - Product: Feature announcements, release notes
4. **Advanced workflows**:
   - Content repurposing: 1 blog → 15 social posts (automated)
   - A/B testing: Generate 5 variations for email subject lines
   - Bulk generation: Create month of content in one session
5. **Measure business impact**:
   - Lead generation from content (attributed)
   - Engagement rates (AI vs manual)
   - Cost per content piece (before vs after)
   - Team satisfaction and retention

**Success Metrics**:
- Sustained time savings: 20-30 hours/month
- Output at target level: 80-100 pieces/month
- Quality maintained: 7-8/10 with minimal editing
- Business impact: 15-25% increase in content-driven leads

## Collaboration Best Practices

### Shared Template Library

**Create templates for each content type**:
1. **LinkedIn Thought Leadership Post**
   - Prompt: "Write a LinkedIn post about [topic] for [target audience]. Include a hook, 3 key insights, and a call-to-action. Tone: [professional/casual/inspirational]. Length: 150-200 words."
2. **Blog Post Outline**
   - Prompt: "Create an outline for a blog post about [topic]. Include: Introduction, 5 main sections with subheadings, conclusion. Target audience: [persona]. Goal: [educate/persuade/entertain]."
3. **Email Newsletter Section**
   - Prompt: "Write a newsletter section about [topic]. Include a compelling headline, 2-3 paragraphs, and a CTA. Tone: friendly and informative. Length: 150 words."
4. **Ad Copy Variations**
   - Prompt: "Generate 5 Facebook ad headlines and 3 ad body variations for [product/service]. Target audience: [persona]. Focus on benefit: [primary value prop]."

**Template Management**:
- Store templates in shared Google Doc or team wiki
- Organize by content type (social, blog, email, ads)
- Update based on what works (track performance)
- Version control (date updated, who created)

### Approval Workflow

**Standard Review Process**:
1. **Content Creator**: Generate draft in AI tool (5-10 min)
2. **Self-Edit**: Quick review for accuracy and voice (5 min)
3. **Submit for Review**: Share link or export to Google Doc
4. **Manager Review**: Check for brand alignment, quality (5-10 min)
5. **Revisions**: Creator makes edits based on feedback (5 min)
6. **Approve & Publish**: Manager approves, creator schedules

**Time per Piece**: 20-30 minutes total (vs 60-90 minutes manually)

**Tools for Workflow**:
- Jasper: Built-in collaboration and comments
- Copy.ai: Export to Google Docs for review
- Asana/Monday: Create task with link to draft
- Slack: Quick approval for time-sensitive content

### Brand Voice Training

**Best Practices for Team Consistency**:
1. **Upload 15-20 samples**: Best-performing content across all types
2. **Document voice characteristics**:
   - Tone: Professional but approachable
   - Language: Industry-specific but accessible
   - Sentence structure: Short, punchy vs long, detailed
   - Perspective: First-person plural ("we") vs second-person ("you")
3. **Create voice guide**: 1-page reference for team
4. **Regular updates**: Refresh voice profile quarterly with new high-performers
5. **Team training**: 30-minute session on brand voice expectations

**Example Voice Profile**:
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS
- **Tone**: Professional, helpful, slightly playful
- **Avoid**: Jargon, overly formal language, hype
- **Prefer**: Clear explanations, real examples, benefits-focused
- **Sentence length**: Mix of short (10-15 words) and medium (20-25 words)

## Common Challenges and Solutions

### Challenge 1: "Our team won't adopt the tool"

**Root Cause**: Change resistance, unclear value proposition

**Solutions**:
1. **Pilot with champions**: Start with 1-2 enthusiastic team members
2. **Show immediate value**: Generate first week of content in 30 minutes
3. **Training**: 1-hour hands-on session (not lecture)
4. **Make it easy**: Pre-load templates, provide cheat sheet
5. **Celebrate wins**: Share time saved and quality improvements

**Timeline**: 80% adoption within 4-6 weeks if properly managed

### Challenge 2: "Quality is inconsistent across team members"

**Root Cause**: Different skill levels with prompting and editing

**Solutions**:
1. **Standardize prompts**: Use shared template library (no custom prompts initially)
2. **Review first 10 pieces**: Manager provides feedback to each team member
3. **Pair experienced with new**: Buddy system for learning
4. **Quality checklist**: 5-point checklist before submitting for review
5. **Ongoing training**: Monthly 30-min workshop on best practices

**Timeline**: Consistency improves 60-80% within 2-3 months

### Challenge 3: "Manager becomes approval bottleneck"

**Root Cause**: All content requires manager review before publishing

**Solutions**:
1. **Graduated permissions**: Experienced team members get "publish" rights after 3 months
2. **Spot-check system**: Manager reviews 20-30% of content randomly
3. **Pre-approval**: Template-based content pre-approved (just fill in details)
4. **Clear guidelines**: Document what needs review vs what can auto-publish
5. **Batch review**: Manager reviews 10-15 pieces in one 30-min session

**Timeline**: Bottleneck reduced by 50-70% within 2 months

### Challenge 4: "We're not sure if AI content performs well"

**Root Cause**: No data tracking or comparison

**Solutions**:
1. **Tag content**: Label posts as "AI-assisted" in backend (not public)
2. **A/B test**: Run AI vs manual content on same platforms
3. **Track metrics**: Engagement, click-through, conversions
4. **Monthly report**: Compare AI vs manual performance
5. **Iterate**: Double-down on what works, refine what doesn't

**Industry Data**: AI-assisted content performs 10-20% better (when properly edited and personalized)

### Challenge 5: "Our brand voice is getting diluted"

**Root Cause**: Inconsistent use of brand voice profile, insufficient editing

**Solutions**:
1. **Refresh voice profile**: Update with latest high-performing content
2. **Voice audit**: Manager reviews all content types for consistency
3. **Team alignment**: 1-hour voice workshop with examples
4. **Stricter review**: Temporarily increase manager review % (50-75%)
5. **Editing guidelines**: Document specific edits needed for voice alignment

**Timeline**: Voice consistency improves 40-60% within 1 month
