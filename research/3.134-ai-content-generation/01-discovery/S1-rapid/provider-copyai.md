## Key Features

### Social Media Content Generation
- **Platforms Supported**: Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, Facebook
- **Content Types**: Captions, hooks, hashtags, post ideas, campaign concepts
- **Workflow**: Input topic/brief → AI generates multiple variations → Edit and publish

### Content Repurposing Workflows
- **Blog-to-Social**: Convert long-form articles into multiple social posts
- **Multi-Platform Optimization**: Automatically adjust tone and format for each platform

### Collaboration Features
- **Team Workspaces**: Shared access to projects and templates

## Use Cases for Consultants

### Ideal For:
- **Social media automation**: Daily LinkedIn/Twitter posts
- **Content repurposing**: Blog posts → multiple social formats
- **Quick copy generation**: Client proposals, email outreach
- **Brand consistency**: Maintaining voice across platforms

### Workflow Example (Consultant Use Case):
1. Write one case study or thought leadership article (manually or with AI assist)
2. Use Copy.ai workflow to generate:
   - 5 LinkedIn posts (different angles)
   - 10 Twitter/X posts (key insights)
   - 3 email newsletter snippets
   - 2 Instagram captions (if applicable)
3. Review and edit (10-15 minutes)
4. Schedule with social media tool (Buffer, Hootsuite)
5. **Time Saved**: 3-4 hours per week
