## Comparison to Alternatives

### vs Copy.ai
- **Writesonic Better**: Long-form content, SEO, analytics
- **Copy.ai Better**: Social media focus, simpler interface, repurposing workflows
- **Price**: Similar (Copy.ai $49/mo vs Writesonic $39-79/mo)

### vs Jasper
- **Writesonic Better**: Price ($39 vs $49), multi-model access, integrations
- **Jasper Better**: Brand voice training, content quality (subjective), team features
- **Price**: Writesonic cheaper ($39 vs $49)

### vs API (Claude/GPT-4)
- **Writesonic Better**: No coding, built-in SEO, templates, analytics
- **API Better**: Cost at scale ($3/mo vs $39/mo), full customization
- **Price**: API much cheaper at volume

### vs Rytr
- **Writesonic Better**: SEO features, integrations, multi-model access, long-form
- **Rytr Better**: Price ($29 vs $39), simplicity, unlimited plan
- **Price**: Rytr cheaper ($29 vs $39)
