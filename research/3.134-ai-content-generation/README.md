# 3.134: AI Content Generation & Copywriting

## Research Overview

**Research Question**: What are the best AI content generation tools for consultants and small businesses? SaaS platforms vs API-based approaches?

**Target Users**: Consultants and small businesses (1-5 person teams)

## Use Cases

- Social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
- Blog content drafting
- Marketing copy
- Content repurposing pipelines (long-form → 10-15 social posts)

## Methodology

Following **MPSE v3.0** structured research approach:

### S1: Rapid Discovery (CURRENT)
- **Status**: Complete
- **Duration**: 60-90 minutes
- **Focus**: Platform comparison, pricing analysis, initial recommendations
- **Deliverables**: Provider profiles, comparison matrix, recommendation summary

### Future Stages (As Needed)
- **S2**: Comprehensive Discovery - Deeper feature analysis, testing
- **S3**: Need-Driven Discovery - Specific workflow implementation

## Key Research Questions

1. **Pricing comparison**: What's the break-even point for API-based ($0.02-0.10/piece) vs SaaS ($49/mo)?
2. **Quality control**: How do platforms handle brand voice consistency?
3. **Content repurposing**: Which tools best support "1 blog post → 10 social posts" workflow?
4. **Integration**: How do these connect to 3.135 social media scheduling tools?
5. **Prompt engineering**: How much customization is available?
