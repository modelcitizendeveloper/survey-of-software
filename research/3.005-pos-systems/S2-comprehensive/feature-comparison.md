## Conclusion

### Feature Leaders by Category

**Overall Features (Most Complete):**
1. Lightspeed (Retail or Restaurant) - Most features built-in
2. Toast (Restaurant) - Best for restaurant operations
3. Shopify POS (Retail + E-commerce) - Best omnichannel

**Best for Budget (Free/Low Tier):**
1. Square - Most features in free plan
2. Shopify POS Lite - Good if you already have Shopify store

**Best Customization:**
1. Clover - Extensive app marketplace
2. Shopify POS - 8,000+ Shopify apps

**Best Offline Mode:**
1. TouchBistro - Hybrid cloud + local server
2. Clover - Configurable offline transaction limits

**Best Restaurant Features:**
1. Toast - Most comprehensive
2. TouchBistro - Close second, better offline
3. Lightspeed Restaurant - Excellent for inventory-focused restaurants

**Best Retail Features:**
1. Lightspeed Retail - Best inventory management
2. Shopify POS - Best omnichannel (online + offline)
3. Square for Retail - Best ease of use
