# PostHog

## Description

PostHog is an open-source product analytics platform that combines analytics, session recording, feature flags, and A/B testing in a single platform. It can be self-hosted or used as a cloud service, providing full control over data.

## Open Source Status

**GitHub Stars**: 29,565+ stars
**License**: MIT (self-hosted version)
**Repository**: https://github.com/PostHog/posthog

## Pricing

**Free Tier (Cloud):**
- 1M events/month free
- 5k session recordings/month
- 1M feature flag requests/month
- 100k error exceptions/month
- 1,500 survey responses/month

**Pay-As-You-Go:**
- Usage-based pricing beyond free tier
- Can set billing limits to avoid surprises
- 90%+ of companies use PostHog for free

**Enterprise:**
- Starts at ~$2,000/month
- Custom contracts
- Advanced security and compliance features

## Setup Time Estimate

**Time to First Event**: 15-30 minutes
- Quick SDK integration (JavaScript, Python, Node.js, etc.)
- Auto-capture available for web apps
- Self-hosting adds 30-60 minutes (Docker/Kubernetes)

## Key Differentiator

**Open-source flexibility with comprehensive features**: PostHog is the only major product analytics platform that offers full self-hosting capabilities under MIT license, making it ideal for teams requiring data sovereignty or wanting to avoid vendor lock-in. The generous free tier (1M events) makes it accessible for startups while scaling to enterprise needs.

## Best For

- Teams requiring self-hosted analytics
- Companies with data sovereignty requirements
- Startups wanting comprehensive analytics without upfront costs
- Developer-focused teams comfortable with open-source tools
