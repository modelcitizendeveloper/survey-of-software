# Mixpanel

## Description

Mixpanel is a leading product analytics platform focused on event-based tracking and user behavior analysis. Known for its powerful funnel analysis and segmentation capabilities, it's widely adopted across SaaS companies.

## Open Source Status

**Not open source** - Proprietary SaaS platform

## Pricing

**Free Tier:**
- Up to 1M events/month
- 10k basic session replays
- Unlimited seats
- Core reports: Insights, Funnels, Retention, Flows

**Growth Plan:**
- Starts at $20/month for starter features
- $140/month for 1.5M events
- $2,289/month for 20M events
- Includes cohorts, custom properties, saved metrics

**Enterprise:**
- Starts at $833/month
- Unlimited events
- Advanced analytics and governance
- Premium support

**Startup Program:**
- First year free for qualifying startups
- $50k credit (covers up to 150M events/month)
- Requirements: < 5 years old, < $8M funding

## Setup Time Estimate

**Time to First Event**: 20-30 minutes
- Straightforward SDK integration
- Good documentation
- Web, mobile, and backend SDKs available

## Key Differentiator

**Best-in-class funnel analysis with transparent pricing**: Mixpanel pioneered event-based analytics and remains the gold standard for conversion funnel analysis. Its transparent, event-based pricing model and generous free tier make it accessible for teams of all sizes, while the startup program offers exceptional value for early-stage companies.

## Best For

- SaaS companies focused on conversion optimization
- Teams analyzing complex user journeys
- Startups qualifying for the startup program
- Product teams needing self-service analytics
