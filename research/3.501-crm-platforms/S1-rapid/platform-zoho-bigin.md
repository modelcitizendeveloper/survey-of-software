# Zoho Bigin - Lightweight CRM Profile

**Market Segment**: Lightweight
**G2 Rating**: 4.3/5
**Pricing**: $7-15/user/month
**Market Position**: ~2% SMB market share

---

## Quick Take

Zoho Bigin is a simplified, pipeline-centric CRM designed specifically for small businesses and startups. It's the most affordable full-featured CRM in this category.

**Best For**: Solo to 10-person teams needing simple pipeline management without complexity

**Not For**: Teams needing advanced marketing automation or enterprise features

---

## Key Strengths

✅ **Lowest pricing** in lightweight category ($7/month)
✅ **Built-in telephony** included at base price
✅ **Workflow automation** in Express plan
✅ **Upgrade path** to full Zoho CRM as you grow
✅ **Simple interface** - no learning curve

---

## Key Weaknesses

❌ Limited to 6 pipelines
❌ Fewer integrations than competitors
❌ Part of Zoho ecosystem (can be overwhelming if you don't use other Zoho apps)

---

## Integration Ecosystem

**Strong**: Email (Gmail, Outlook), Calendar, Zoho suite
**Adequate**: Third-party via Zapier
**Weak**: Native POS integrations

**Rating**: Strong (especially within Zoho ecosystem)

---

## Lock-in Risk

**Risk Level**: Low

**Why**:
- Standard CSV data export
- API access available
- No long-term contracts required
- Easy migration path to Zoho CRM or competitors

**Escape Cost**: $500-2,000 (migration to another lightweight CRM)

---

## Pricing Details

**Express**: $7/user/month
- 6 pipelines
- Workflow automation
- Built-in telephony
- Email insights
- Basic reporting

**Premier**: $12/user/month
- Everything in Express
- Advanced reports
- Custom functions
- SalesSignals

**Enterprise**: $15/user/month
- Everything in Premier
- Multi-pipeline view
- Team collaboration features

**Annual billing**: 20% discount

---

## Use Cases

✅ **Wine bar B2B sales** (10-50 wholesale accounts)
✅ **Startup SaaS** (50-200 customers)
✅ **Service business** (client management)
✅ **Solo consultant** (simple pipeline)

❌ **E-commerce** (better tools exist)
❌ **Enterprise sales** (too simple)
❌ **Marketing-heavy business** (lacks features)

---

## Comparison Notes

**vs Pipedrive**: Bigin is ~50% cheaper but less visual, fewer integrations
**vs Close**: Bigin lacks calling power features, but way more affordable
**vs Copper**: Bigin has better automation, Copper has better Google integration

**Migration from**: Spreadsheet, manual process
**Migration to**: Zoho CRM, Pipedrive, HubSpot (when you outgrow)

---

## Strategic Assessment

**2025 Viability**: Strong - Zoho is well-funded, actively developing
**Acquisition Risk**: Low - part of larger Zoho Corp ecosystem
**Feature Roadmap**: Steady improvements, following full Zoho CRM

**Recommendation**: Excellent choice for budget-conscious small teams needing basic CRM without complexity.
