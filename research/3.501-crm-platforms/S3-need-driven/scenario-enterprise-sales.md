# Scenario: Enterprise Sales Team (100+ Reps, Complex Deals)

**Business Pattern**: Large sales organization with complex, multi-stage deal cycles

---

## Context

**Customer Base**: 500-10,000+ enterprise accounts

**Team**: 100+ sales reps + managers + operations

**Budget**: $15,000-50,000+/month for CRM

**Sales Motion**: 6-12 month cycles, multiple stakeholders, custom approvals

**Complexity**: Territories, forecasting, integrations, compliance

---

## Recommended Platform

### Primary: Salesforce Enterprise ($165-330/user/month)

**Why**: Industry standard, infinitely customizable, dedicated admin team justifies investment

---

### Alternative: HubSpot Enterprise ($3,600/month)

**When to choose**: Marketing-led enterprise sales, easier to use than Salesforce

---

## Key Decision

**Do you have**:
- 100+ salespeople ✅
- Dedicated Salesforce admin ✅
- Complex custom processes ✅
- Budget for implementation ($50K-500K) ✅

**If YES to all** → Salesforce
**If NO to any** → You're not ready for enterprise CRM yet

---

## Applies To

- Enterprise software companies
- Large B2B manufacturers
- Financial services firms
- Multi-national corporations
