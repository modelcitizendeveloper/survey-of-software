# S2: Comprehensive Analysis Approach
**Time Budget**: 30-40 minutes
**Goal**: Deep feature-by-feature comparison within market segments

---

## Methodology

### 1. Segmented Comparisons (25 min)
Rather than comparing Zoho Bigin to Salesforce (nonsensical), create apples-to-apples comparisons within segments:

- **Lightweight segment**: Zoho Bigin vs Pipedrive vs Close vs Copper
- **Mid-market segment**: HubSpot vs Zoho CRM vs Freshsales
- **Enterprise segment**: Salesforce vs Microsoft Dynamics (note: limited scope for enterprise)

### 2. Feature Matrices (10 min)
Create standardized feature comparison tables:
- Core CRM features (contacts, deals, pipeline)
- Automation capabilities
- Integration ecosystem
- Reporting/analytics
- Mobile capabilities
- Support tiers

### 3. Pricing Deep-Dive (5 min)
- Total cost of ownership (TCO) analysis
- Hidden costs (storage, API calls, support)
- Volume pricing and discounts
- Annual vs monthly pricing

---

## Output Structure

**Segment Comparison Documents:**
- `lightweight-comparison.md` - Detailed Bigin/Pipedrive/Close/Copper
- `midmarket-comparison.md` - Detailed HubSpot/Zoho CRM/Freshsales
- `enterprise-notes.md` - Brief Salesforce/Dynamics notes (not full comparison)

**Cross-Cutting Analysis:**
- `pricing-matrix.md` - TCO across all platforms
- `integration-ecosystem.md` - Integration quality ratings

---

## Key Questions to Answer

1. **Within lightweight**: What justifies Pipedrive's 2x price premium over Bigin?
2. **Within mid-market**: When does HubSpot's lock-in risk outweigh convenience?
3. **Across segments**: At what point do you outgrow lightweight and need mid-market?
4. **Hidden costs**: What are the true costs beyond sticker price?

---

**Time Budget**: 30-40 minutes total
**Deliverable**: Segmented feature matrices + pricing analysis
