## Strategic Decision Tree: In-House AI Capabilities

This section helps you decide your strategy based on whether your organization has (or plans to build) in-house AI/ML capabilities.

---

### Path A: No In-House AI Capabilities (Most Organizations)

**Strategy: Maximize Platform AI, Buy Third-Party Add-Ons**

#### Platform Selection Priority:
1. **Best AI coverage:** Salesforce Einstein (70-80% coverage)
2. **Good AI + affordability:** HubSpot (50-60%, but Enterprise tier expensive), Freshsales (45-55%, best mid-market AI)
3. **Acceptable:** Zoho CRM (35-45% coverage, budget-friendly)

#### What to Build vs Buy:
| Capability | Strategy | Solution |
|------------|----------|----------|
| **Lead scoring** | Buy platform | Salesforce Einstein, HubSpot Enterprise, Freshsales |
| **Deal scoring** | Buy platform | Salesforce Einstein, HubSpot Enterprise, Freshsales |
| **Email optimization** | Buy platform | Salesforce, HubSpot, Freshsales all have this |
| **Activity capture** | Buy platform | All platforms do this well (Copper best for Gmail) |
| **Sales forecasting** | Buy platform | Salesforce leads, others have basic |
| **Churn prediction** | Build or buy third-party | Gainsight, ChurnZero, or custom ML |
| **Sentiment analysis** | Build or buy third-party | Custom LLM layer or specialized tools |
| **Email generation** | Buy third-party or build | Custom LLM (GPT-4, Claude) |
| **Relationship intelligence** | Buy third-party | Affinity, People.ai, or custom graph analysis |

#### Decision Framework:
```
Q1: What's your budget for CRM + AI?
  <$1K/month ‚Üí Freshsales or Zoho CRM (good AI at affordable price)
  $1K-$5K/month ‚Üí HubSpot (comprehensive but expensive) or Freshsales (better value)
  >$5K/month ‚Üí Salesforce Einstein (best AI, highest cost)

Q2: Which AI capabilities matter most?
  Lead/deal scoring ‚Üí Salesforce Einstein or Freshsales
  Marketing automation + AI ‚Üí HubSpot
  Gmail-native + basic AI ‚Üí Copper
  Budget + decent AI ‚Üí Zoho CRM or Freshsales

Q3: For gaps, can you buy third-party tools?
  Yes ‚Üí Supplement CRM with ChurnZero, Gong.io, People.ai, etc.
  No ‚Üí Accept platform limitations or reconsider Path B
```

#### Action Plan:
1. Select AI-rich CRM based on budget (Salesforce > HubSpot > Freshsales > Zoho)
2. Identify critical gaps (churn prediction, advanced sentiment, relationship intelligence)
3. Search for third-party AI tools to fill gaps (Gainsight, Gong.io, People.ai)
4. Accept remaining gaps as unavailable until market matures

### Path B: Have In-House AI/ML Capabilities (Data Science Team, ML Engineers)

**Strategy: Use CRM for Basics, Build Custom AI for High-Value Gaps**

#### Platform Selection Priority:
1. **API access is critical** - prioritize CRMs with robust APIs:
   - **Best APIs:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Freshsales
   - **Good APIs:** Pipedrive, Close
   - **Limited APIs:** Copper (Google-focused), Zoho Bigin

2. **Don't overpay for platform AI** - if you're building custom, choose based on:
   - API quality and completeness
   - Data export capabilities
   - Total cost (don't pay for Salesforce Einstein if you're building your own)

#### What to Build vs Buy:
| Capability | Strategy | Why Build Custom |

| **Lead scoring** | Buy if using Salesforce, build otherwise | Salesforce Einstein is good, others are basic |

| **Sentiment & conversation intelligence** | **Build custom** | Platforms are basic, LLM + ML superior |
| **Relationship intelligence** | **Build custom** | 0% platform coverage, graph analysis + LLM powerful |
| **Advanced forecasting** | **Build custom** | Platforms have basic, custom ML models superior |
| **Deal scoring** | Buy if using Salesforce, build otherwise | Custom models can outperform platform |
| **Activity capture** | Buy platform | Platforms do this well, not worth custom |
| **Email optimization** | Buy platform, enhance | Platforms have basics, add LLM subject line generation |
