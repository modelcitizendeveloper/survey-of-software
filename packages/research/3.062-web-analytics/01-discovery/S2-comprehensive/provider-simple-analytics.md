## Overview

Simple Analytics is a privacy-first, cookie-free web analytics platform launched in 2018. It positions itself as the cleanest, simplest analytics tool with AI-driven insights and strong GDPR compliance documentation. Known for transparent privacy policies that satisfy legal teams.

**Market Position:** Privacy-first analytics with emphasis on legal compliance clarity and simplicity.

**Philosophy:** "Privacy-friendly by design, no consent pop-ups needed."

---

## Core Features

### Basic Analytics
- Pageviews, unique visitors, referrers, top pages
- Country-level geographic data
- Device, browser, OS tracking
- Real-time visitor tracking

### Advanced Capabilities
- Event tracking with custom dimensions
- Goals dashboard for conversions
- API access (higher tiers)
- Role-based views and permissions
- AI-driven insights and reports
- CSV export

### Integrations
- WordPress, Ghost, Hugo plugins
- Framework integrations (Next.js, Nuxt, etc.)
- Email reports

### Limitations
- No user-level tracking (privacy by design)
- No city-level geo data
- Basic segmentation only
- No funnels or cohort analysis
