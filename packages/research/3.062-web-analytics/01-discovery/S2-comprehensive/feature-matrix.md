## Quick Selection Guide

**Choose based on priorities:**

1. **Privacy-First + Simple:**
   - Plausible (best balance)
   - Fathom (ad-blocker bypass)
   - Cloudflare (free)

2. **Full Features + Free:**
   - Google Analytics 4 (if GDPR okay)
   - PostHog (if product analytics)

3. **Privacy + Advanced Features:**
   - Matomo (self-hosted or cloud)
   - Piwik PRO (enterprise only)

4. **Free + Self-Hosted:**
   - Umami (simplest)
   - Matomo (most features)
   - PostHog (product analytics)

5. **Enterprise + GDPR:**
   - Piwik PRO
   - Matomo On-Premise
   - Adobe (if budget allows)
