# Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

**Category:** Full-featured
**Website:** https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/
**Pricing:** Free (with paid GA4 360 for enterprise)

## Key Features
- Event-based tracking for granular user interactions
- Cross-platform and cross-device measurement
- Privacy controls including cookie-less measurement options
- Machine learning insights and predictive analytics
- Integration with Google Ads and marketing ecosystem

## Best For
Businesses already in the Google ecosystem who need comprehensive analytics with strong marketing attribution capabilities. Suitable for small businesses to large enterprises.

## Quick Notes
- Most widely used web analytics platform globally
- Free tier sufficient for most small-to-medium businesses
- Significant learning curve compared to simpler alternatives
- Privacy concerns for EU/GDPR-sensitive audiences
- Replaced Universal Analytics in 2023, now the standard Google offering
