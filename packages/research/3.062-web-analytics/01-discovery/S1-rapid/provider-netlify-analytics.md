# Netlify Analytics

**Category:** Developer-focused / Platform-integrated
**Website:** https://www.netlify.com/products/analytics/
**Pricing:** $9/month per site (add-on to Netlify hosting)

## Key Features
- Server-side analytics (no client-side JavaScript)
- No cookie banners or privacy concerns
- Captures all traffic including bots
- Built-in to Netlify platform
- Forms, identity, and split testing integration

## Best For
Netlify users who want server-side analytics without client-side tracking scripts or privacy compliance concerns.

## Quick Notes
- Unique server-side approach (no JS required)
- Batteries-included with forms and identity features
- More comprehensive than Vercel's offering
- Requires Netlify hosting platform
- Premium feature at $9/month per site
