# GoatCounter

**Category:** Privacy-focused / Open-source / Self-hosted
**Website:** https://www.goatcounter.com/
**Pricing:** Free (donation-supported hosted) or self-hosted

## Key Features
- Privacy-aware, no unique identifiers or personal data
- No GDPR notice required
- Lightweight (~3.5KB script)
- Identify unique visits without cookies
- Completely open-source (MIT license)

## Best For
Indie developers, personal sites, and small projects wanting free, privacy-friendly analytics with minimal setup.

## Quick Notes
- Created by Martin Tournoij as indie project
- Free hosted service supported by donations
- Can be self-hosted without restrictions
- Written in Go
- Active development (latest version June 2025)
- Popular in open-source and indie developer communities
