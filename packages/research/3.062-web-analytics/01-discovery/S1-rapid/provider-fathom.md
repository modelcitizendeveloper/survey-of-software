# Fathom Analytics

**Category:** Privacy-focused
**Website:** https://usefathom.com/
**Pricing:** Starting at $14/month (up to 100K pageviews)

## Key Features
- Cookie-less tracking, no consent banners needed
- Simple setup (takes minutes)
- GDPR, CCPA, ePrivacy, PECR compliant
- Fast loading times, minimal performance impact
- Email reports and custom dashboards

## Best For
Small to medium businesses and content creators who prioritize user privacy and want straightforward analytics without complexity.

## Quick Notes
- Positions itself as "better Google Analytics alternative"
- Focus on simplicity and ease of use
- Strong reputation in privacy-conscious developer community
- Canadian company with EU data hosting options
- No cookies or personal information stored
