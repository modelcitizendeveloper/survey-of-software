## Red Flags / Gotchas

### 1. "AI Will Solve Everything"
- **Reality**: AI needs 2-4 weeks to calibrate (first 2 weeks = mediocre)
- **Expectation**: Perfect scheduling from day 1
- **Solution**: Be patient, adjust preferences, give AI time to learn

### 2. "Too Many Calendar Blocks"
- **Problem**: Every task = calendar event ‚Üí 50 blocks/day = overwhelming
- **Reality**: Only schedule priority tasks, keep admin off calendar
- **Solution**: Tune settings, use minimum duration thresholds

### 3. "AI Schedules During Focus Time, But Meeting Invited"
- **Problem**: AI blocks 9-11 AM for deep work, meeting invite arrives for 10 AM
- **Reality**: Either decline meeting or override AI (manual decision)
- **Solution**: Configure "focus time" as hard blocks, decline conflicts

### 4. "Integration Overload"
- **Problem**: Syncing Todoist + Asana + Linear + Gmail = conflicts, duplicates
- **Reality**: More integrations = more complexity
- **Solution**: Start with 1 task source, expand gradually

### 5. "Free Tier ‚Üí Paid Upgrade Shock"
- **Problem**: Reclaim free (10 tasks/week) ‚Üí need Pro ($8/month) = sticker shock
- **Reality**: $8/month = 0.2 hours saved at $50/hour (breaks even if saves 10 min/month)
- **Solution**: Calculate ROI (time saved √ó hourly rate)
