## Executive Summary

**S3 Research Question**: "Which AI productivity tool is best for MY specific use case?"

**Key Findings**:

1. **Persona matching critical**: ADHD users need Motion/Reclaim (autopilot), visual thinkers need Trevor
2. **Workload complexity drives choice**: <10 tasks = free tier, 30+ tasks = Motion
3. **Reclaim = most versatile**: Works for 70-80% of use cases (managers, remote workers, budget-conscious)
4. **Motion = specialized**: Best for ADHD, founders, complex projects (20-30% of users)
5. **Trevor/Sunsama = niche**: Only for users who value manual control > automation

## Workload Complexity Mapping

### Simple (<10 tasks/week): Free Tiers
- **Reclaim Free**: $0/month (unlimited habits + 10 tasks/week)
- **Todoist Free**: $0/month (unlimited tasks, no AI scheduling)
- **Verdict**: Reclaim Free = best for light users (has AI scheduling)

### Moderate (10-30 tasks/week): Reclaim Pro
- **Reclaim Pro**: $8/month (unlimited tasks, AI scheduling, best value)
- **Trevor AI**: $10/month (visual alternative, manual placement)
- **Verdict**: Reclaim Pro = best ROI (30-60×)

### Complex (30-50+ tasks): Motion
- **Motion**: $34/month (project dependencies, deadline propagation)
- **Alternative**: Reclaim Pro + Todoist = $13/month (cheaper, but no dependencies)
- **Verdict**: Motion justified if projects have dependencies

## Team Size Recommendations

### 2-5 Users: Individual Plans
- **Cost**: 5 × $8 (Reclaim) = $40/month
- **When to upgrade**: Need shared calendar, team no-meeting days

### 5-20 Users: Team Plans
- **Reclaim Business**: $10/user/month (calendar-heavy teams)
- **Motion Teams**: $34/user/month (project-heavy teams)
- **Decision**: Meeting-heavy → Reclaim, Project-heavy → Motion

### 20-50 Users: Evaluate Build vs Buy
- **Threshold**: 50 users × $34 = $1,700/month = $20,400/year
- **Custom build**: $50-100k one-time + $5-10k/year maintenance
- **Break-even**: 3-5 years (see S4 for full analysis)

## Migration Path Recommendations

### Easiest Migration: Todoist → Reclaim
- **Effort**: 15-30 minutes (no task migration, just connect)
- **Result**: Keep Todoist (tasks), add Reclaim (AI scheduling)
- **Cost**: +$8/month (Reclaim Pro)

### Highest ROI Migration: Trevor/Sunsama → Reclaim/Motion
- **Savings**: $1,500-3,000/year time cost (eliminate daily manual workflow)
- **Effort**: 1-2 hours
- **Why**: AI auto-scheduling eliminates 10-15 min/day planning ritual

### Hardest Migration: Motion → Reclaim
- **Effort**: 2-3 hours (lose project dependencies)
- **Savings**: $22-26/month = $264-312/year
- **Trade-off**: Cost savings vs losing advanced AI features

## S3 Key Insights

### 1. Persona > Price
- ADHD users: Motion ($34/month) worth 10× the cost (prevents ADHD tax: missed deadlines, lost opportunities)
- Budget-conscious: Reclaim Free ($0/month) sufficient for light users
- **Implication**: Match tool to need, not price (ROI varies by persona)

### 2. Reclaim = 70-80% Use Cases
- Works for: Managers, remote workers, budget-conscious, moderate workloads
- Doesn't work for: Complex projects (no dependencies), ADHD (less autopilot than Motion)
- **Implication**: S1 recommendation (Reclaim for most people) validated by S3

### 3. Motion = Specialized (20-30% Use Cases)
- Works for: ADHD, founders, complex projects, power users
- Overkill for: Simple workflows, meeting-heavy managers, budget-constrained
- **Implication**: Motion premium ($34/month) justified for specific personas

### 4. Trevor/Sunsama = Niche (<10% Use Cases)
- Works for: Visual thinkers (Trevor), mindfulness-focused (Sunsama)
- Fails for: ADHD (too manual), managers (too time-consuming), founders (too reactive)
- **Implication**: High time cost ($1,500-3,000/year) limits adoption

### 5. Team Plans = Undervalued
- Reclaim Business: $10/user/month → 1-2 hours saved/user/month = 10-20× ROI
- Motion Teams: $34/user/month → 2-3 hours saved/user/month = 6-9× ROI
- **Implication**: Team adoption should be faster (ROI compelling)
