# BlueCart - B2B Ordering & Inventory Management Platform

**Category**: SaaS - B2B Ordering Platform with Inventory Management
**Target Market**: Restaurants, food distributors, supplier networks
**Pricing**: Starting from $0.01 (marketplace model, tiered pricing available)
**Network Size**: 125,000+ restaurants, thousands of vendors
**Status**: Active, rapidly growing platform

---

## Overview

BlueCart is a B2B eCommerce platform connecting restaurants with food distributors and suppliers. It started as an ordering marketplace and expanded to include inventory management, making it unique in combining supplier ordering with internal inventory tracking. The 2025 release includes predictive ordering and menu profitability benchmarking.

## Core Features

### Predictive Ordering (NEW 2025)
- **AI-powered demand forecasting**: Historical depletion + seasonality trends
- **Automatic recommendations**: What and when to order
- **Par level automation**: Adjusts based on actual usage vs. theoretical
- **Waste reduction**: Reduces over-ordering and food waste significantly

### Inventory Management
- Real-time inventory tracking with threshold alerts
- Push notifications when stock reaches minimum levels
- Full inventory history and audit trail
- Automatic inventory updates when delivery arrives
- Recipe explosion and ingredient tracking

### Ordering & Procurement
- **One-click repeat ordering**: Save orders with regular suppliers
- **Multi-vendor marketplace**: Order from thousands of suppliers
- **Automatic item addition**: New products added to inventory on delivery
- **Mobile ordering**: iOS and Android apps for on-the-go procurement

### Menu Profitability Benchmarking (NEW 2025)
- **Peer comparison**: Benchmark menu items against 125,000 restaurant network
- **POS integration**: Syncs sales data for profitability analysis
- **Data-backed decisions**: Identify underperforming menu items

### Vendor Payment Tools (NEW 2025)
- Flexible payment options (credit, ACH, net terms)
- Real-time invoice tracking
- Cash flow planning tools

### Integrations
- **Accounting**: QuickBooks Online, Xero, NetSuite
- **Inventory**: Fishbowl, Canopy
- **Payments**: Stripe
- **POS**: Various POS systems for sales data sync

## Pricing Structure

**Starting Price**: $0.01 (marketplace access)

**Pricing Model**: Flexible tiered pricing based on usage and features

**Free Trial**: Available

**Estimated Full Feature Pricing**: Industry sources suggest $100-200/month for inventory + ordering features

**Unique Value**: Access to supplier network may reduce food costs through competitive pricing

## Strengths

✅ Massive supplier network (125,000+ restaurants, thousands of vendors)
✅ 2025 AI features (predictive ordering, profitability benchmarking) industry-leading
✅ Combines ordering + inventory in single platform
✅ Low entry price point ($0.01 marketplace access)
✅ Mobile app for field ordering
✅ Peer benchmarking unique to BlueCart network
✅ Free trial reduces adoption risk

## Limitations

❌ Inventory features less mature than specialized inventory platforms
❌ Best value when ordering through BlueCart suppliers (ecosystem lock-in)
❌ Newer platform vs. established inventory players
❌ May not support all regional distributors
❌ Limited recipe costing vs. MarketMan/Craftable

## Best For

- **Restaurants wanting supplier diversity** (multiple vendor access)
- **Operations optimizing procurement costs** through competitive bidding
- **Budget-conscious startups** (low entry price, free trial)
- **Restaurants with 20-50 menu items** needing basic inventory
- **Teams valuing peer benchmarking** (menu profitability vs. network)
- **Mobile-first operations** (food trucks, catering)

## Not Ideal For

- Restaurants with exclusive distributor contracts
- Advanced recipe costing and variance analysis needs
- Very large multi-location enterprises (unproven at scale)
- Operations requiring deep POS integration
- Regions with limited BlueCart supplier coverage

## Decision Factors

**Choose BlueCart if**:
- Want access to multiple suppliers for price comparison
- Need predictive ordering to reduce food waste
- Budget <$150/month for inventory + ordering
- Value peer menu profitability benchmarking
- Mobile ordering is important (delivery, catering)

**Skip BlueCart if**:
- Locked into exclusive distributor relationships
- Need advanced recipe costing and variance tracking
- Require deep POS integration for COGS
- Prefer established enterprise-grade platforms
- Regional suppliers not on BlueCart network
