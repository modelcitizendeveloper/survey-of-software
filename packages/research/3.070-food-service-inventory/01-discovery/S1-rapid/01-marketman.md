# MarketMan - Restaurant Inventory Management Platform

**Category**: SaaS - Comprehensive Inventory & Back-of-House Management
**Target Market**: Multi-location restaurants, ghost kitchens, hospitality groups
**Pricing**: Custom quote (3 tiers: Starter, Growth, Enterprise)
**Year Founded**: 2013
**Status**: Active, market leader

---

## Overview

MarketMan is a cloud-based inventory management and purchasing solution that automates back-of-house operations, delivering fast, accurate reporting on inventory, COGS (Cost of Goods Sold), and expenses. The platform is designed for restaurants of all sizes, from single locations to multi-location enterprise operations.

## Core Features

### Inventory Management
- Cloud-based real-time inventory tracking
- Automated COGS calculations
- Food costing and menu profitability analysis
- Waste tracking (spoilage, over-portioning, theft)
- Variance analysis (theoretical vs. actual usage)
- Par level management with automated alerts

### AI-Powered Recipe Management
- AI tool creates new recipes in minutes from screenshot of ingredients list
- Automatic ingredient matching to existing inventory
- Recipe explosion (ingredient breakdown per menu item)
- Recipe costing and margin analysis

### Ordering & Purchasing
- AI-driven ordering forecasts demand based on historical usage
- Automated vendor data sync (prices, product codes)
- Purchase order generation and tracking
- Multi-vendor order consolidation

### Invoice Processing
- Automated invoice digitization and data extraction
- Real-time invoice tracking with payment status
- Integrated vendor payments (credit, ACH)
- Automatic credit updates

### Integrations
- POS integrations (Toast, Square, Lightspeed, etc.)
- Distributor EDI integrations (automatic price updates)
- Accounting software integrations (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Orders and invoices flow directly into system

### Multi-Location Management
- Centralized "HQ" account manages all locations
- Cross-location reporting and analytics
- Standardized recipes and ordering across locations
- Location-specific customization where needed

✅ Comprehensive feature set covering entire back-of-house operation
✅ AI-powered recipe creation and demand forecasting (industry-leading)
✅ Strong POS and distributor integrations
✅ Multi-location management with centralized control
✅ Automated invoice processing reduces manual data entry
✅ Mature product with large customer base

## Limitations

❌ Pricing not transparent (requires sales consultation)
❌ Higher price point than single-purpose solutions
❌ May be overkill for small single-location restaurants
❌ Tiered invoice limits could be constraining for high-volume operations

## Best For

- **Multi-location restaurant groups** needing centralized control
- **Ghost kitchen operations** managing multiple brands
- **High-volume restaurants** processing many invoices monthly
- **Operations prioritizing automation** over manual processes
- **Teams seeking comprehensive solution** vs. point solutions

## Not Ideal For

- Very small restaurants (<$500K annual revenue)
- Single-location cafes with simple inventory needs
- Budget-conscious operations wanting transparent pricing
- Restaurants preferring modular vs. all-in-one approach

## Decision Factors

**Choose MarketMan if**:
- Managing 3+ locations that need standardization
- Processing 50+ invoices/month and need automation
- Have budget for premium solution ($200-400/mo)
- Want AI-powered forecasting and recipe management
- Need tight POS integration for COGS tracking

**Skip MarketMan if**:
- Single location with <20 menu items
- Limited budget (<$150/month)
- Need transparent pricing without sales calls
- Prefer open-source or self-hosted solutions

## ROI Potential

Based on industry benchmarks:
- Food waste reduction: 2-6% of food costs
- For $1.5M revenue restaurant (30% food cost = $450K)
- 4% waste reduction = $18,000/year savings
- Platform cost: ~$3,600/year ($300/month)
- **Net ROI**: $14,400/year or 400% return
