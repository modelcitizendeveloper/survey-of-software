## Overview

This document assesses long-term vendor viability and AI evolution trajectories for 8 FP&A platforms:

1. **Financial health**: Public, PE-backed, VC-backed viability
2. **5-year acquisition predictions**: M&A likelihood, potential acquirers
3. **10-year survival probability**: Platform longevity assessment
4. **Roadmap stability**: Public vs startup risk
5. **Customer base momentum**: Growth vs decline indicators
6. **5-Year AI Evolution Outlook (2025-2030)**: Commoditization timeline, differentiation analysis

**Philosophy**: Vendor selection is a 5-10 year commitment. Choose vendors likely to survive and evolve.

## Roadmap Stability Assessment

### Public Companies: Predictable (Low Volatility)

**OneStream, Adaptive (Workday)**:
- **Release cadence**: 2-3 major releases/year (quarterly earnings pressure)
- **Feature velocity**: Moderate (incremental improvements, not radical pivots)
- **Predictability**: High (public roadmaps, customer advisory boards)
- **Risk**: Low (established platforms, large customer bases = conservative changes)

**Example**: Adaptive adds AI features over 3 years (2023-2025), not overnight
- 2023: Predictive AI beta
- 2024: General availability
- 2025: Enhanced AI features

**Verdict**: Choose public companies for predictable, stable roadmaps

---

### PE-Backed: Aggressive Growth (Moderate Volatility)

**Anaplan, Planful, Vena, Prophix**:
- **Release cadence**: 3-4 major releases/year (PE pressure to grow)
- **Feature velocity**: High (aggressive feature launches, rapid iteration)
- **Predictability**: Moderate (PE exit pressure = feature bloat risk)
- **Risk**: Moderate (PE exit may change strategy post-sale)

**Example**: Planful launches 4 major features in 2025 (Consolidations Premium, Workforce Pro, AI Assistant, Enhanced Integrations)
- **Positive**: Fast innovation (competitive advantage)
- **Negative**: Feature bloat (complexity increases, user experience suffers)

**Verdict**: PE-backed platforms innovate fast but may over-extend

---

### VC-Backed Startups: High Agility (High Volatility)

**Runway, Causal (pre-acquisition)**:
- **Release cadence**: 4-6 major releases/year (startup speed)
- **Feature velocity**: Very high (rapid iteration, customer feedback loops)
- **Predictability**: Low (pivot risk, competitive pressure)
- **Risk**: High (VC pressure = rapid changes, some features abandoned)

**Example**: Runway launched AI Copilot (July 2024), Ambient Intelligence (same year)
- **Positive**: Fast AI innovation (beat competitors by 12-18 months)
- **Negative**: Beta features (not production-proven, may change significantly)

**Verdict**: VC-backed platforms innovate fastest but highest roadmap volatility

## 5-Year AI Evolution Outlook (2025-2030)

### Current State (2025): AI Experimentation Phase

**AI Features Available Today**:
- **Tier 1 (Production)**: Runway Ambient Intelligence, Adaptive Predictive AI, Anaplan PlanIQ
- **Tier 2 (Beta)**: Planful Plan Assistant, OneStream AI Agents, Prophix AI, Causal AI
- **Tier 3 (None)**: Vena (no documented AI features)

**AI Pricing**: 10-30% premium (Runway Copilot, Planful AI Assistant)

**AI Maturity**: Early (most features <2 years old, limited customer feedback)

---

### 2025-2026: AI Becomes Table Stakes

#### What Will Happen

**All Platforms Add AI Features**:
- **Vena**: Announces AI features (2026 roadmap, likely leverage Microsoft Copilot)
- **Causal**: LucaNet integrates AI into consolidated platform
- **Adaptive**: Enhances Workday ML integration (deeper AI across Workday ecosystem)

**AI Pricing Shifts**:
- **2025**: +20-30% subscription premium for AI (early adopter tax)
- **2026**: +10-15% premium (competitive pressure drives down pricing)
- **2027**: Included in base subscription (commoditized)

**AI Accuracy Benchmarks Emerge**:
- Industry standards for AI forecast accuracy (MAPE, RMSE)
- False positive rates for anomaly detection
- User satisfaction scores for variance explanations

**Beta → Production Maturity**:
- Planful Plan Assistant moves to GA (general availability)
- OneStream AI Agents move to GA
- 12-24 months customer feedback validates AI ROI

---

#### What Will Commoditize by 2026-2027

**Natural Language Queries** (90% commoditized by 2027):
- **Today**: Only Runway, Planful, OneStream offer (premium feature)
- **2027**: All platforms offer (table stakes)
- **Example**: "Show me Q3 budget variance by department" (every platform can do this)

**Variance Explanations** (80% commoditized by 2027):
- **Today**: Runway Ambient Intelligence (unique)
- **2027**: All platforms auto-generate variance explanations
- **Differentiation**: Accuracy and relevance (generic vs company-specific)

**Narrative Generation** (70% commoditized by 2027):
- **Today**: Runway, Planful offer (beta/production)
- **2027**: All platforms auto-write board deck commentary
- **Differentiation**: Writing quality (generic boilerplate vs insightful)

---

#### What Remains Differentiated (2026-2027)

**Predictive Forecasting (ML-based)** (50% adoption by 2027):
- **Leaders**: Adaptive (Workday ML), Anaplan (PlanIQ)
- **Laggards**: Runway, Planful (LLM-based, less mature than ML)
- **Differentiation**: Forecast accuracy (Adaptive/Anaplan 7+ years production, others <2 years)
