- **Anomaly detection**: Identify unusual patterns in revenue/expenses

- **Regression analysis**: Predict outcomes based on historical patterns
- **Predictive modeling**: Multi-variable forecasting
- **Natural language queries**: "What's Q4 revenue forecast for Product A?"

**AI Finance Assistant** (Conversational Co-Pilot):
- Ask questions in plain English: "Why did Q3 revenue miss forecast?"
- Instant answers without building reports
- Variance explanations generated automatically
- KPI summaries on demand

**AI-Predictive Forecasting** (Dynamic):
- Continuously adapts to revenue signals (pipeline changes, bookings velocity)

**Flexible Structure**:
- Add new products **without rebuilding entire model** (vs. Anaplan model complexity)
- New markets/geographies/segments added dynamically
- No consultant-driven customization required
- Business structure changes don't break models

**Why It Matters for FP&A**:
- Growth-stage companies change structure frequently (new products, markets)
- Anaplan/Planful: $50K-200K customization costs for model changes
- Una: Self-service model updates (finance team owns, not IT)

### Performance Planning (Planning + Execution)

**Execution Tracking**:
- Assign tasks to departments/owners
- Track plan vs. actuals with accountability
- Workflow automation (approval chains, alerts)
- **"Performance Planning"** = plans grounded in execution reality

**Accountability Framework**:
- Who owns each budget line?

- Progress tracking on initiatives
- **Not just "what if" planning** - "how to achieve" execution

**Strategic Assumptions Testing**:

**Comparative Analysis**:

### Budgeting & Forecasting

**Dynamic Budgeting**:
- Budget updates without full re-plan

**Variance Analysis**:
