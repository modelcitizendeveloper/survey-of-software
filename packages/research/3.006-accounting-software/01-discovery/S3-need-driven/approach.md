# S3: Need-Driven Discovery - Approach

**Goal:** Match specific business scenarios to optimal accounting software solutions

**Time Budget:** 2-3 hours

**Methodology:**
1. Define 15-20 realistic business scenarios
2. Match each scenario to best-fit platform
3. Provide reasoning for recommendation
4. Include runner-up options
5. Highlight critical decision factors

---

## Scenario Categories

### By Business Model
- Freelancer/solo consultant
- Service-based businesses (agencies, professional services)
- Product-based businesses (retail, e-commerce, wholesale)
- SaaS and subscription businesses
- Hybrid businesses (product + service)
- Manufacturing and distribution
- Nonprofits and associations

### By Business Size
- Micro ($0-$100K revenue)
- Small ($100K-$1M revenue)
- Growing ($1M-$5M revenue)
- Mid-market ($5M-$100M revenue)

### By Complexity
- Single entity, simple operations
- Multi-location, single entity
- Multi-entity, need consolidation
- International operations
- Complex project accounting

---

## Scenario Template

For each scenario:
- **Business Profile:** Description of business type, size, needs
- **Primary Recommendation:** Best platform for this scenario
- **Rationale:** Why this platform wins
- **Runner-Up:** Alternative option
- **Budget Alternative:** Lower-cost option
- **Red Flags:** When NOT to choose this option
- **Migration Path:** What to do as you grow
