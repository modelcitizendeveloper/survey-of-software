# S1 Rapid Discovery: AI Meeting Transcription & Note-Taking Tools

**Research ID**: 3.136
**Research Date**: November 20, 2025
**Research Duration**: 60-90 minutes
**Methodology**: MPSE v3.0 S1 Rapid Discovery

---

## Research Scope

Evaluate AI-powered meeting transcription tools for automating meeting documentation, generating summaries, and extracting action items.

### In Scope
- Automated meeting transcription services
- AI-powered summaries and action items
- Integration with video conferencing platforms (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet)
- CRM and productivity tool integrations
- Privacy and data policies

### Out of Scope
- Manual transcription services (human-powered)
- Audio/video editing tools
- Meeting scheduling software (see 3.133 AI Productivity)
- Project management platforms (see 3.131 Team Task Management)

---

## Providers Evaluated

This S1 research covers 4 major providers:

1. **Fireflies.ai** - Privacy-focused, 60+ languages, strong CRM integrations
2. **Otter.ai** - Budget-friendly, real-time transcription, accessibility features
3. **Grain** - Customer insights, video clips, trend tracking
4. **Fathom** - Generous free tier, simplicity-focused

---

## Key Research Questions

1. What is the pricing range across providers? (free tiers vs paid plans)
2. Which providers have the strongest privacy policies? (AI training, data retention)
3. What integration ecosystems exist? (CRM, PM tools, Zapier)
4. How accurate is transcription across providers?
5. What languages are supported beyond English?
6. What meeting platforms are supported?
7. When does it make sense to use these tools vs manual note-taking?

---

## Research Approach

**Step 1: Provider Identification**
- Identify market-leading providers via web search
- Validate provider maturity (customer base, funding, reviews)

**Step 2: Feature Mapping**
- Pricing tiers (free, paid, enterprise)
- Core features (transcription, summaries, action items)
- Integrations (meeting platforms, CRM, PM tools)
- Privacy policies (AI training, data retention, compliance)

**Step 3: Comparison Matrix**
- Create side-by-side feature comparison
- Identify price/value leaders
- Map use cases to optimal providers

**Step 4: Recommendations**
- Best for budget
- Best for privacy
- Best for integrations
- Best for specific use cases (solo consultant, customer-facing, international)
