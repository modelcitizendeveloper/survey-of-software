# TouchBistro - S1 Rapid Profile

## Overview

**Provider:** TouchBistro Inc.
**Market Share:** Niche leader in full-service restaurant segment

## Market Position

TouchBistro is a **full-service restaurant specialist**, competing directly with Toast in the table-service dining segment. Known for its iPad-based POS with robust offline capabilities (hybrid cloud + local server).

**Recognition:**
- Popular among independent full-service restaurants
- Strong in casual dining and fine dining segments
- Known for reliable offline mode
- Trusted by thousands of restaurants globally

## Key Strengths

### 1. Hybrid Cloud + Local Server (Best Offline Mode)
- **Fully functional offline:** Unlike purely cloud-based competitors
- **Local server option:** Keeps restaurant running during internet outages
- **Automatic sync:** When connection restored
- **Critical for restaurants:** Can't afford to stop serving due to internet issues

### 2. Full-Service Restaurant Features
- **Table management:** Visual floor plans, table status, server sections
- **Tableside ordering:** iPads for servers to take orders at the table
- **Split bills:** By item, by seat, custom splits
- **Course timing:** Fire courses strategically (appetizers, then entrees)
- **Menu modifiers:** Extensive customization options
- **Tip management:** Pooling, distribution, reporting

### 3. iPad Flexibility
- Works with standard iPads (not proprietary like Toast)
- Use existing iPads or purchase new
- Easier to replace if damaged (buy any iPad)
- Lower hardware lock-in than Toast

### 4. No Commission on Online Ordering
- **TouchBistro Online Ordering:** Built-in, no commission fees
- Unlike third-party delivery (DoorDash, etc.) which charge 15-30%
- Customers order directly from restaurant
- Flat monthly fee instead of per-order commission

### 5. Reservation Integration
- **TouchBistro Reservations:** Built-in reservation system
- Flat monthly fee, no per-reservation charges
- Alternative to OpenTable (which charges per cover)
- Integrates with POS for table management

### 6. Payment Processor Choice
- Not locked to single processor
- Can shop around for better rates
- Switch processors without changing POS
- More flexibility than Toast or Square

## Key Weaknesses

### 1. Opaque Pricing
- Entry price public ($69/mo), but most pay more
- Must contact sales for detailed quotes
- Add-ons not transparently priced
- Difficult to estimate true total cost upfront

### 2. Many Features Are Add-Ons
- **Online ordering:** Additional monthly fee
- **Reservations:** Additional monthly fee
- **Loyalty:** Additional cost
- **Gift cards:** Additional fee
- **Advanced inventory:** May require higher tier
- Base price low, but total cost rises with add-ons

### 3. iPad-Only (No Android)
- Must use iPads, not Android tablets
- More expensive than Android alternatives
- Limits hardware flexibility
- iPad ecosystem requirement

### 4. Smaller Ecosystem Than Toast/Square
- Fewer integrations available
- Smaller app marketplace
- Less third-party developer activity
- May lack niche integrations competitors offer

### 5. Local Server Complexity (Optional)
- Strongest offline mode requires local server
- Additional hardware cost ($500-$1,000)
- More setup complexity
- Requires technical setup or installer

### 6. Support and Documentation
- Some users report inconsistent support quality
- Documentation less extensive than larger competitors
- Smaller company means fewer support resources
