# S1-Rapid: Approach

## Methodology

The **S1-Rapid** approach focuses on quickly identifying the most popular and widely-adopted POS systems in the market. This methodology is designed for businesses that need a fast overview of proven solutions with strong market presence.

## Research Strategy

### 1. Market Share Analysis
Identify platforms with the largest market share and customer base:
- Industry reports and market research data
- Customer counts and adoption rates
- Year-over-year growth trends

### 2. Recognition and Reviews
Evaluate platforms based on:
- G2, Capterra, and Software Advice ratings
- Number of reviews (indicates adoption scale)
- Overall satisfaction scores

### 3. Quick Differentiation
For each major platform, identify:
- **Primary strength:** What the platform is best known for
- **Target market:** Which business types use it most
- **Price positioning:** Budget-friendly vs premium
- **Key differentiator:** What makes it unique

## Selection Criteria

Platforms included in S1-Rapid must meet these thresholds:
- **Significant market presence:** 1,000+ business customers
- **Multi-industry support:** Used across different business types
- **Established track record:** 3+ years in market
- **Current availability:** Actively sold and supported in 2025

## What S1-Rapid Provides

### Quick Reference Information
- Platform name and parent company
- Market position and share (if available)
- Target business types
- Starting price point
- Payment processing rates
- Key strengths and weaknesses
- Best fit scenarios

### Rapid Decision Framework
- **Need general-purpose POS fast?** → Square
- **Running a restaurant?** → Toast or TouchBistro
- **Retail + e-commerce?** → Shopify POS
- **Need extensive customization?** → Clover
- **Multi-location retail?** → Lightspeed
- **Mobile/pop-up business?** → Square, SumUp, or Zettle
- **Service business (salon/spa)?** → Square Appointments or Vagaro

## Limitations of S1-Rapid

This rapid analysis provides:
- ✅ Quick overview of major players
- ✅ Market share and positioning data
- ✅ High-level feature differentiation
- ✅ Starting price points

This rapid analysis does NOT provide:
- ❌ Detailed feature-by-feature comparisons
- ❌ Total cost of ownership (TCO) analysis
- ❌ Contract terms and lock-in assessment
- ❌ Integration capability deep-dive
- ❌ Migration complexity evaluation

**For comprehensive analysis, see S2-Comprehensive.**
**For use-case specific guidance, see S3-Need-Driven.**
