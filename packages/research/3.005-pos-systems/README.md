## What is a POS System?

A Point of Sale (POS) system combines hardware and software to enable businesses to:
- **Accept payments** (card-present, contactless, mobile wallets, cash)
- **Manage inventory** (track stock, variants, suppliers, multi-location)
- **Manage staff** (permissions, time tracking, scheduling, payroll)
- **Track customers** (CRM, loyalty programs, purchase history)
- **Generate reports** (sales, inventory, staff performance, analytics)
- **Integrate operations** (accounting, e-commerce, delivery apps)

See `DOMAIN_EXPLAINER.md` for comprehensive explanation of POS systems, components, business models, and industry trends.

## Key Comparisons

### Square vs Toast (General vs Restaurant Specialist)
- **Square:** Easier, cheaper, more flexible (month-to-month), better for QSR and general-purpose
- **Toast:** More restaurant features, better KDS, better for full-service, 24-month contract

### Square vs Shopify POS (General vs E-commerce Hybrid)
- **Square:** Better standalone POS, simpler, cheaper if no e-commerce
- **Shopify:** Unmatched online/offline integration, best for omnichannel brands

### Toast vs TouchBistro (Restaurant Leaders)
- **Toast:** Larger ecosystem, more transparent pricing, better delivery integration
- **TouchBistro:** Best offline mode, standard iPads, processor choice flexibility

### Clover vs Square (Customization vs Simplicity)
- **Clover:** More customizable (app marketplace), better offline mode, often requires contracts
- **Square:** Simpler setup, more transparent pricing, month-to-month, better ecosystem

## Critical Considerations

### Payment Processing Lock-In
**Locked (Must Use Vendor's Processor):**
- Square ‚Üí Square Payments (2.6% + 10¬¢)
- Toast ‚Üí Toast Payments (2.49% + 15¬¢)
- Shopify ‚Üí Shopify Payments (2.4-2.7%, or +2% fee for third-party)

**Flexible (Processor Choice):**
- TouchBistro ‚Üí Choose from partners
- Lightspeed ‚Üí Can bring your own (may incur extra fees)

### Hardware Lock-In
**Proprietary (Locked to Vendor):**
- Toast ‚Üí Toast-branded hardware ($800-$1,500 per device)
- Clover ‚Üí Clover devices ($749-$1,799)

**Standard Devices (Portable):**
- Square ‚Üí Use smartphone or purchase Square hardware
- Lightspeed ‚Üí Standard iPads or Android tablets
- TouchBistro ‚Üí Standard iPads
- Shopify ‚Üí Various standard devices

### Contract Terms
**Month-to-Month (No Commitment):**
- Square, Shopify POS, Lightspeed

**Typically Requires Contract:**
- Toast (24 months typical)
- Clover (36 months via resellers often)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Choosing based on lowest monthly fee only**
   ‚Üí Calculate total cost (software + processing at your volume)

2. **Not testing during free trial**
   ‚Üí Process real transactions, train staff, evaluate usability

3. **Ignoring contract terms**
   ‚Üí Check for early termination fees, auto-renewal, commitment length

4. **Overlooking data export capabilities**
   ‚Üí Verify you can export customer/sales data if you switch later

5. **Underestimating add-on costs**
   ‚Üí List ALL features needed, get total cost including add-ons

6. **Choosing general-purpose POS for specialized needs**
   ‚Üí Match POS to business type (Toast for restaurants, Lightspeed for inventory-heavy retail)
