# Pendo

## Description

Pendo is a comprehensive Software Experience Management (SXM) platform that combines product analytics with in-app guidance, user feedback, and product roadmapping. It goes beyond pure analytics to enable product teams to act on insights directly within the application.

## Open Source Status

**Not open source** - Proprietary SaaS platform

## Pricing

**Free Tier:**
- Up to 500 monthly active users (MAU)
- Retroactive product analytics
- In-app guides
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Product roadmaps
- All-in-one platform access

**Base/Core:**
- Starts at $7,000/year
- Custom pricing beyond starter tier
- Standard annual increases: 5-20% (unless multi-year contract)

**Pulse/Ultimate:**
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Advanced AI capabilities
- Specialized AI Agents and Agent Analytics
- Cross-app executive dashboards
- Premium support

## Setup Time Estimate

**Time to First Event**: 30-45 minutes
- Single script installation
- Automatic event capture
- Additional time needed to configure in-app guides
- More complex setup than pure analytics tools

## Key Differentiator

**Unified analytics + in-app guidance platform**: Pendo's key differentiation is seamlessly combining robust product analytics with actionable in-app guidance, feedback mechanisms, and roadmapping. Rather than just showing you what users do, Pendo enables you to guide them directly within your product, making it a complete Software Experience Management platform rather than just an analytics tool.

## Best For

- B2B SaaS companies wanting analytics + user onboarding
- Product teams needing to guide users without engineering resources
- Organizations wanting unified analytics and feedback tools
- Enterprises requiring comprehensive product experience management
