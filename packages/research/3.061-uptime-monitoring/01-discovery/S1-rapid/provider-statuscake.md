# StatusCake
## Comprehensive Free Tier with Multiple Monitor Types

### Market Position
- Established uptime monitoring provider (10+ years)
- UK-based company with global presence
- Strong focus on small business and freelancers
- Not open source (proprietary SaaS)

### Free Tier Details

**Monitor Limits:**
- 10 uptime monitors
- 1 page speed monitor (24-hour intervals)
- 1 domain monitor (weekly checks)
- 1 SSL certificate monitor

**Check Intervals:**
- 5-minute intervals for uptime checks
- Page speed: Once per 24 hours
- Domain/SSL: Once per week

**Alerting:**
- Email alerts: Unlimited (free)
- SMS alerts: Paid feature
- Integrations: Available (Slack, webhooks, etc.)
- Multiple alert contacts supported

**Status Pages:**
- Not included in free tier
- Available on paid plans only

**Data Retention:**
- Historical uptime data included
- Reporting dashboard access

**Monitoring Types:**
- HTTP(s) monitoring
- TCP port monitoring
- DNS monitoring
- SSL certificate expiry tracking
- Page speed monitoring

**Monitoring Locations:**
- Multiple global locations
- Configurable per monitor

### Paid Tier Pricing

**Superior Plan: $20.41/month**
- 25 uptime monitors @ 1-minute intervals
- 5 page speed monitors (6-hour intervals)
- 5 domain monitors
- 5 SSL monitors
- Status page included

**Business Plan: $56.25/month**
- 100 uptime monitors
- 30-second check intervals
- Priority support
- Advanced reporting

**Premium Plan: Custom pricing**
- Unlimited monitors
- 10-second intervals
- Dedicated support
- SLA guarantees

### Key Differentiator
Only free tier offering SSL certificate monitoring, domain monitoring, and page speed checks alongside uptime monitoring - providing comprehensive website health visibility at zero cost.

### Best For
- Small businesses needing SSL/domain monitoring
- Freelancers managing client websites
- Projects requiring comprehensive free monitoring
- Users who don't need status pages immediately
