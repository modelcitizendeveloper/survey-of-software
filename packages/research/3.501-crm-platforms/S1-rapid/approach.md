# S1: Rapid Discovery Approach
**Time Budget**: 15-20 minutes
**Goal**: Quick scan of CRM landscape, identify top platforms by popularity

---

## Methodology

### 1. Market Leaders Identification (5 min)
- Search G2/Capterra CRM rankings
- Identify top 10 by user count and ratings
- Filter by use case (lightweight, mid-market, enterprise)

### 2. Quick Feature Scan (5 min)
- Core features: Contact mgmt, pipeline, automation, reporting
- Integration ecosystem: Email, calendar, accounting, POS
- Pricing transparency: Clear pricing page vs "contact sales"

### 3. Red Flags Check (3 min)
- Recent bad reviews (product issues, pricing changes, support problems)
- Acquisition risk (recently acquired, product sunset)
- Lock-in indicators (poor data export, proprietary API)

### 4. Popularity Ranking (2 min)
- Order by: Market share, user reviews, ecosystem adoption
- Segment: Lightweight vs Mid-market vs Enterprise
