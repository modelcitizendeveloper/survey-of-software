# Close CRM - Lightweight CRM Profile

**Market Segment**: Lightweight (Premium)
**G2 Rating**: 4.6/5 (highest in category)
**Pricing**: $25-125/user/month
**Market Position**: ~1% SMB market, 10K+ businesses

---

## Quick Take

Close is a sales engagement platform disguised as a CRM. Built for outbound sales teams who live on the phone and email. Best-in-class calling features, but expensive.

**Best For**: Inside sales teams doing high-volume cold calling and email outreach (5-50 reps)

**Not For**: Budget-conscious teams, inbound-focused sales, businesses without heavy calling needs

---

## Key Strengths

✅ **Best calling features** - power dialer, predictive dialer, call recording, coaching
✅ **Built-in communication** - calling, email, SMS all native (no integrations needed)
✅ **Email sequences** - sophisticated multi-touch automation
✅ **Highest G2 rating** (4.6) and user satisfaction (9.3/10)
✅ **Sales engagement** - combines CRM + outreach tools

---

## Key Weaknesses

❌ **Highest price** in lightweight category ($25-125 vs $7-15 for Bigin)
❌ **No marketing features** - pure sales tool
❌ **Overkill for simple needs** - 80% of features unused by most SMBs
❌ **Learning curve** - more complex than Pipedrive or Bigin

---

## Integration Ecosystem

**Strong**: Email (Gmail, Outlook), Calendar, Zapier
**Adequate**: Third-party via API
**Weak**: Marketing tools (by design - not a marketing CRM)
**None**: Native POS, e-commerce

**Rating**: Adequate (intentionally focused on sales communication)

---

## Lock-in Risk

**Risk Level**: Medium

**Why**:
- Communication history lives in Close (hard to export call recordings, sequences)
- Email sequences not portable to other platforms
- Training investment (steeper learning curve)
- Team becomes dependent on calling features

**Escape Cost**: $3,000-8,000 (data migration + retrain team + rebuild sequences)

---

## Pricing Details

**Startup**: $25/user/month (3 users minimum, annual billing)
- Power dialer
- Email sequences (5 active)
- Predictive email
- Basic reporting
- 1,000 contacts
- **Missing**: SMS, call coaching, advanced features

**Professional**: $59/user/month
- Everything in Startup
- SMS capabilities
- 25 active email sequences
- Call coaching
- Advanced reporting
- 10,000 contacts
- Custom fields

**Business**: $99/user/month
- Everything in Professional
- Bulk email
- Predictive dialer
- 100 email sequences
- Team management
- 100,000 contacts

**Enterprise**: $125/user/month (custom pricing for 10+ users)
- Everything in Business
- Dedicated support
- Custom onboarding
- Advanced security

**Note**: 3-user minimum even for Startup plan ($75/month minimum)

## Use Cases

✅ **Outbound sales team** (SDRs making 50+ calls/day)
✅ **Inside sales** (phone + email heavy)
✅ **B2B SaaS** (cold outreach, demo scheduling)
✅ **Sales agency** (volume calling for clients)

❌ **Small B2B operation** (10-50 accounts, not worth $75+/month minimum)
❌ **Inbound-only sales** (paying for features you won't use)
❌ **Small team on budget** (too expensive)
❌ **Marketing-led business** (no marketing features)

---

## Comparison Notes

**vs Pipedrive**: Close wins on calling, loses on visual pipeline and marketing
**vs Bigin**: Close is 3-5x more expensive, only worth it if you LIVE on the phone
**vs Copper**: Close better for calling, Copper better for Gmail-native workflow

**Unique Value**: If your team makes >30 calls/day per rep, Close pays for itself in efficiency. If not, you're overpaying.

**Migration from**: Pipedrive (if adding serious calling), spreadsheet (if sales team)
**Migration to**: Salesforce (if going enterprise + need more features)

---

## Strategic Assessment

**2025 Viability**: Strong - VC-backed (lead by Salesforce Ventures), focused product
**Acquisition Risk**: Medium - VC-backed (could be acquired by larger CRM)
**Feature Roadmap**: AI-powered sequences, better analytics, sales intelligence

**Pricing Trajectory**: Stable - hasn't had major increases, but 3-user minimum limits flexibility

**Recommendation**: Only choose Close if calling is your primary sales motion. If you're making <20 calls/day per person, Pipedrive or Bigin will serve you better at half the cost.

---

## When Close Makes Sense

**ROI Calculation**:
- If calling saves 1 hour/day per rep → $25/month = $0.10/hour (worth it)
- If calling saves 2 hours/week per rep → $25/month = $2.50/hour (not worth it)

**Decision Rule**: If your sales process is 70%+ phone/email outreach, Close. Otherwise, Pipedrive or Bigin.
