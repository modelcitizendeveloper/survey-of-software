# Mid-Market CRM: Comprehensive Comparison
**Platforms**: HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Freshsales
**Segment**: 200-2,000 customers, 10-100 person teams, $0-3,600/month

---

## Executive Summary

**HubSpot**: Best all-in-one (marketing + sales + CRM), but high lock-in
**Zoho CRM**: Best value - full features at 1/3 HubSpot cost
**Freshsales**: Best AI features - modern UX, Freshworks ecosystem

## When to Choose Each

### HubSpot
**Choose if**:
- Marketing automation is core (content, SEO, lead nurturing)
- Want all-in-one platform (CRM + marketing + sales + service)
- Can afford $1,600+/month OR willing to start with free tier
- Inbound-led business model

**Avoid if**:
- Pure sales team (paying for marketing features you won't use)
- Worried about lock-in (HubSpot is hardest to leave)
- Budget-conscious (gets expensive fast)

**Small B2B operation** (10-50 accounts): ❌ Overkill

---

### Zoho CRM
**Choose if**:
- Need full CRM features at affordable price
- Already use Zoho ecosystem (Books, Desk, etc.)
- Upgrade path from Zoho Bigin
- Want flexibility without lock-in

**Avoid if**:
- Need best-in-class marketing automation (HubSpot better)
- Want cutting-edge AI features (Freshsales better)

**Small operation growing**: ⚠️ Could upgrade from Bigin if accounts grow to 200+

---

### Freshsales
**Choose if**:
- Want modern, AI-powered sales features
- Part of Freshworks ecosystem (Freshdesk, etc.)
- Need intelligent lead scoring and email suggestions
- Want alternative to HubSpot/Salesforce with better UX

**Avoid if**:
- Marketing automation is priority (HubSpot better)
- Budget very tight (Zoho CRM cheaper)
- Small team (<10 people) - lightweight CRM sufficient

**Small operation**: ❌ Overkill and more expensive than needed

## Pricing Deep-Dive

### HubSpot Reality Check

**Free**: Great for testing, limited for scaling
**Starter** ($20/month + contact fees): Very limited, most outgrow quickly
**Professional** ($1,600/month): Where real value starts - 3 seats + 2,000 contacts
**Enterprise** ($3,600/month): 5 seats + 10,000 contacts

**Hidden Cost**: Contact-based pricing means you pay more as you grow (by design)

**Total Cost (10 users, 5,000 contacts, 1 year)**: $25,000-35,000

---

### Zoho CRM Simple Pricing

**Standard**: $14/user/month
**Professional**: $23/user/month (most popular)
**Enterprise**: $40/user/month
**Ultimate**: $52/user/month

**Total Cost (10 users, unlimited contacts, 1 year)**: $2,760 (Professional)

**Value**: 1/10th HubSpot cost for comparable CRM features (minus marketing automation)

---

### Freshsales Straightforward

**Growth**: $15/user/month
**Pro**: $39/user/month
**Enterprise**: $69/user/month

**Total Cost (10 users, 1 year)**: $4,680 (Pro)

---

## Recommendation

**For Mid-Market Teams (10-100 people)**:
1. **If marketing-led**: HubSpot (accept lock-in for convenience)
2. **If budget-conscious**: Zoho CRM (best value)
3. **If modern/AI-focused**: Freshsales (good middle ground)

**For small B2B operations** (<200 accounts): None of these - stick with Zoho Bigin until you have 200+ accounts.
