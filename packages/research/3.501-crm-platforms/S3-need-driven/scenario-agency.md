# Scenario: Agency / Professional Services (20-100 Clients)

**Business Pattern**: Service business with ongoing client relationships and project pipelines

---

## Context

**Client Base**: 20-100 active clients (retainer + project-based)

**Team**: 5-20 people (account managers, project teams)

**Budget**: $300-1,500/month for CRM

**Sales Motion**: Relationship-based, proposals, contract renewals

**Tech Stack**: Gmail/Google Workspace, project management tools (Asana, Monday)

---

## Recommended Platform

### Primary: Copper Professional ($69/user/month)

**Why**: Gmail-native workflow saves 1+ hour/day per person

**Best for**: Agencies that live in Gmail

---

### Alternative 1: Pipedrive Professional ($59/user/month)

**When to choose**: Better pipeline features, not Gmail-dependent

---

### Alternative 2: HubSpot Professional ($1,600/month)

**When to choose**: Need marketing automation for agency's OWN lead generation

---

## Migration Path

**Year 1**: Copper or Pipedrive
**Year 2+**: Stay with choice OR upgrade to HubSpot if adding marketing

---

## Applies To

- Marketing agencies, PR firms
- Consulting firms, professional services
- Design/creative agencies
- Law firms, accounting firms (small to mid-size)
