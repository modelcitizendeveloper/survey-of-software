# Vendor Viability Assessment - 5-Year and 10-Year Outlook
## Experiment 3.202: Speech & Audio AI Services

**Research Phase**: S4 - Strategic Selection
**Date**: 2025-11-24
**Purpose**: Assess long-term survival probability for 8 speech/audio AI providers

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis assesses the 5-year and 10-year survival probability for 8 providers across two categories:

**SaaS Meeting Platforms**: Fireflies.ai, Otter.ai, Grain, Fathom
**Speech-to-Text APIs**: OpenAI Whisper, AssemblyAI, Deepgram, Rev AI

**Key Findings**:

| Provider | 5-Year Survival | 10-Year Survival | Primary Risk | Strategic Backing |
|----------|----------------|------------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **OpenAI Whisper** | 95% | 85% | Pivot risk (OpenAI shifts focus) | Microsoft ($13B invested) |
| **AssemblyAI** | 90% | 70% | Competitive pressure | Strong VC ($115M raised) |
| **Deepgram** | 85% | 65% | Enterprise dependence | Strong VC ($86M raised) |
| **Rev AI** | 90% | 75% | Human transcription decline | Profitable, $100M+ ARR |
| **Otter.ai** | 80% | 60% | Freemium unsustainability | Strong VC ($63M raised) |
| **Fireflies.ai** | 75% | 50% | Acquisition likely | Modest VC ($19M raised) |
| **Fathom** | 70% | 45% | Niche market limitations | Recent Series A ($22M raised) |
| **Grain** | 65% | 40% | CRM integration dependency | Modest VC ($20M raised) |

**Strategic Insight**: Corporate-backed platforms (OpenAI Whisper with Microsoft, Rev with profitable parent) show highest 10-year viability. VC-backed startups (Grain, Fathom, Fireflies) face acquisition or consolidation by 2028-2030.

---

## Methodology

**Survival probability** is calculated using weighted scoring across 5 factors:

| Factor | Weight | Scoring Criteria |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| **Funding Strength** | 30% | Total raised, runway estimate, recent funding |
| **Market Position** | 25% | User base, revenue growth, market share |
| **Strategic Backing** | 20% | Corporate parent, independence vs acquisition risk |
| **Revenue/Profitability** | 15% | Revenue, path to profitability, unit economics |
| **Competitive Moat** | 10% | Unique technology, network effects, differentiation |

**5-year survival probability**: Weighted score × 100 (80-100% = high, 50-79% = medium, <50% = high risk)

**10-year survival probability**: 5-year probability × 0.7-0.9 (adjusted for market consolidation, technology shifts)
