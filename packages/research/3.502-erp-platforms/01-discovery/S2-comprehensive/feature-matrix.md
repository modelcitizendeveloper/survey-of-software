# S2 Comprehensive: Feature Matrix

**Date**: November 2, 2025
**Platforms Compared**: 6 ERPs (NetSuite, Dynamics 365 BC, SAP Business One, Odoo, Acumatica, Oracle ERP Cloud)
**Purpose**: Deep feature comparison across 25+ ERP capability areas

---

## Rating System

- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Excellent** - Best-in-class, comprehensive features
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Very Good** - Strong capabilities, minor gaps
- ⭐⭐⭐ **Good** - Adequate for most use cases
- ⭐⭐ **Basic** - Minimal features, may need add-ons
- ⭐ **Weak** - Limited or missing functionality
- ❌ **Not Available** - Feature not included

## Key Takeaways

1. **Acumatica leads overall** (96%) due to excellent balance of features, UX, and flexibility
2. **Dynamics 365 BC best value** (90%) at 40-50% lower cost than NetSuite with comparable features
3. **SAP Business One dominates manufacturing** (15/15) but weak in UX and technical flexibility
4. **Odoo best UX and flexibility** (88%) with modern interface and open-source advantage
5. **NetSuite strong financials** (83%) but dated UI and higher cost drag score down
6. **Oracle ERP Cloud** (86%) scores high but designed for enterprise scale (overkill for most)
